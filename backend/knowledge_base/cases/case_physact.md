# PhysAct

## Общая информация
- Клиент: PhysAct
- Локация: Sweden
- Отрасль: Healthcare
- Формат: Outsource

## Проблема клиента
PhysAct is a digital service to treat mild to moderate depression. The patient undergoes a program of physical activity, theory lessons with daily support

## Решение
The process is seamlessly integrated into employees’ lives through the PhysAct iOS or Android app. Once registered, users are swiftly introduced to their personal physiotherapist. This dedicated professional becomes a guide on the journey to improved well-being. Regular check-in calls take place, providing a consistent avenue for progress evaluation. Moreover, the app features a messaging system for quick queries, ensuring that users are never left in the dark.

Задачи команды: full outsource

## Ключевые фичи
- PhysAct is on a mission to better manage stress in the work force
- through exercises
- education and evidence supported by physiotherapists.


## О компании клиента (web research)
PhysAct is a Swedish digital healthcare company founded in **2020** by **Marcus Eng**, a physiotherapist specializing in orthopaedic treatment ([thehub.io]).

 Public sources describe PhysAct as using “movement to fight stress” and delivering personalized exercise programs developed by physical therapists ([thehub.io]).

 The company is categorized as having **1–10 employees** ([thehub.io]). Revenue figures are **not found**.

 - Around **SEK 5 million** in equity funding is registered with Sweden’s Bolagsverket, including an unannounced round in **September 2023** worth approximately **SEK 2.8 million** ([swedishtechnews.com]). 
 - In addition, **Gorilla Capital** invested in PhysAct as announced in **September 2024**. Gorilla highlighted PhysAct’s “data‑driven approach” and “personalized physiotherapist support” ([gorillacapital.fi]).

 - **Marcus Eng** – Founder & CEO ([digitalwellventures.com]). 
 - **Luke Harte** – Adviser (likely a leadership or senior role) ([thehub.io]).

Beyond the core offering, PhysAct’s SaaS is delivered via app (iOS/Android) and targets stress-related ill-health in employees; includes personalized exercise programs and therapist communication ([thehub.io]).
