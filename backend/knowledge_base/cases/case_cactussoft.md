## О компании клиента (web research)
CactusSoft traces its origins to 2007 as a small, family-owned software development business established in Minsk, Belarus. ([cactus-now.com]) 
 Founders are Vera Reshetina and Andry Reshetin. ([en.everybodywiki.com])

 In 2017, the Belgian company Litussoft acquired CactusSoft, and the headquarters moved to Ghent, Belgium. ([cactus-now.com])

 Cactus positions itself as an "AI‑first leading software engineering company," aiming to accelerate innovation and deliver impactful, long‑lasting software solutions. Its vision emphasizes meaningful outcomes beyond short‑term results and accelerating innovation through talent and AI. ([cactus-now.com])

 Craft.co lists CactusSoft as High‑Tech service provider; employee estimates vary:
 - Crunchbase reports headcount of 51–100. ([crunchbase.com]) 
 - LeadIQ indicates approximately 100 employees globally, with geographic distribution, including ~6 in Belgium. ([leadiq.com])

 - Bram Van Nieuwerburgh – Chairman, Founder, CFO/CEO (Belgian operations) ([cactus-now.com]) 
 - Mario Vanlommel – CCO, responsible for Sales & Marketing and Eastern Europe hub (since 2020) ([cactus-now.com]) 
 - Jose Laffitte – Head of Engineering, leading Spanish hub ([cactus-now.com]) 
 - Diana Schweiger – COO (Chief of Operations), with digital project/program management background ([cactus-now.com])

Beyond the Belgian e-learning platform, CactusSoft offers:
 - Web and mobile application engineering 
 - Digital transformation services 
 - Project management, business analysis, staffing 
 - Serves sectors like real estate, education, healthcare, IoT, fintech ([craft.co])

 CactusSoft enabled the Belgian company Ocular to rapidly launch a hybrid virtual platform during COVID-19 using nearshoring from Belarus, demonstrating speed and cultural integration benefits. ([cactus-now.com])

 Focuses on Western European companies, including SMEs, SaaS firms, tech startups. Offers nearshore teams aligned culturally with clients. Sectors include proptech, mobility, telecom, health tech. ([crunchbase.com])

 Offers flexible billing: per sprint, milestone-based, fixed price, even result-based remuneration tied to customer success. ([goodfirms.co])

According to their website, technologies used include:
 - Mobile: iOS (Swift), Android (Kotlin), Flutter, React Native 
 - Messaging: RabbitMQ, Azure Service Bus, MQTT 
 - Search: Elasticsearch 
 - Testing: SonarQube, Selenium, Robot Framework 
 - AI/ML: Keras, TensorFlow, JAX, Mojo; LLMs via OpenAI API, Mistral, Llama2, Gemini, Langchain, Claude ([cactus-now.com])

 LeadIQ provides global headcount and geographic distribution: ~100 employees across multiple regions; ~6 engineers in Belgium. ([leadiq.com])

- Opened development center in Seville, Spain in October 2021; registered entity in Georgia in April 2022; implemented new branding in 2022 marking 15 years. ([cactus-now.com])

 In a management reshuffle (date unspecified but around 3.6 years ago), new co‑shareholders named: Diana Schweiger and Jose Laffitte joining the Belgian holding’s executive team with ~100 years combined experience. ([cactus-now.com])
