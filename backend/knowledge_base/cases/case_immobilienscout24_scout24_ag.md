## О компании клиента (web research)
ImmoScout24 began in 1998 under the name "EIB – Elektronische Immobilienbörse" and was founded by Arndt Kwiatkowski, Christian Mangstl, and Joachim Schoss ([de.wikipedia.org]).

 The company positions itself as “Germany’s leading real estate marketplace,” aiming to build a real-estate ecosystem and continuously digitize real estate transactions ([immobilienscout24.de]).

 - ImmoScout24 alone has approximately **650–693 employees** as of early to mid‑2024 ([immobilienscout24.de]). 
 - Scout24 SE (parent) has around **1,066 employees** at the end of 2024, rising to **1,116 employees** by June 30, 2025—a 4.7% increase from the end of 2024 ([de.wikipedia.org]). 
 - Revenue: Scout24 SE reported **€566.3 million** for 2024 (+11.2% YoY), and preliminary FY2025 revenue growth is targeted at **12–14%** ([ug.linkedin.com]). 
 - As of Q3 2025, trailing 12-month revenue is approximately **$554 million USD** (~€513 million) ([accio.com]).

 - Scout24 SE leadership includes **Ralf Weitz (CEO)**, **Martin Mildner (CFO)**, and **Gesa Crockford (CCO)**; **Hans‑Holger Albrecht** is the Supervisory Board Chair ([de.wikipedia.org]).

 - Privatized by Deutsche Telekom in 2006; majority stake sold to Hellman & Friedman in 2013 (~€1.5 billion), followed by IPO in October 2015 (~€899 million volume), with Hellman & Friedman and Deutsche Telekom holding significant stakes afterward ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - In 2019, Scout24 divested AutoScout24, FinanceScout24, and Finanzcheck to Hellman & Friedman for €2.9 billion ([en.wikipedia.org]).

- Evolved from listing platform to include services like financing, valuations, and market monitoring ([de.wikipedia.org]). 
 - In 2014, acquired CRM provider Flowfact (becoming a subsidiary) ([de.wikipedia.org]). 
 - In 2020, acquired immoverkauf24; in 2021, acquired vermietet.de; in 2023, acquired Sprengnetter (valuation services); in December 2024, acquired bulwiengesa AG to enhance commercial real estate data & valuation capabilities ([de.wikipedia.org]).

 - January 2025: Partnership with PropTexx to integrate AI-powered virtual staging and refurnishing into ImmoScout24’s platform ([germannewstoday.com]). 
 - Q4/FY 2025: Scout24 added AI-powered features, including voice integration, to Propstack CRM. In January 2026, ImmoScout24 became among the first European real‑estate platforms to launch [specific feature not fully visible] ([scout24.com]).

 - Monthly active users: **~19–20 million** in Germany and Austria ([scout24.com]). 
 - With the acquisition of Spanish portals Fotocasa and Habitaclia in September 2025, Scout24 aims to scale its ecosystem to **28 million unique visitors per month**, including Spanish-speaking agents and buyers ([cincodias.elpais.com]).

 - Subscription-based services for agents; membership upgrades and listing price adjustments generated growth in ImmoScout24's core business, with membership revenue up by 9.2% to €221.6 million in 2021 ([financialreports.eu]). 
 - MieterPlus (tenant-side paid) membership allows early access to rental listings but has attracted criticism for paygating ads; social housing and rentals under €400 cold rent are exempt ([de.wikipedia.org]).

 - PropTexx for AI staging (2025) ([germannewstoday.com]).
