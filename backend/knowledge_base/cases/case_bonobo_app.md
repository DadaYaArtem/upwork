# Bonobo app

## Общая информация
- Клиент: Bonobo app
- Локация: Spain/Germany
- Отрасль: Social

## Проблема клиента
Bring people together and help new students adapt to a new university

## Решение
An application for students who will study at universities in Spain to connect and learn more about a particular university: read reviews, connect with other students from your university, ask about the experience of senior students.

Задачи команды: Mobile development (iOS, Android): React Native + node js

## Ключевые фичи
- You can create offline events yourself and invite other students. That is
- not go to events from the university that are not interesting to you
- but create your own on your favorite topic. It is possible to make them even paid for a small cost. In case of any inconvenience there is a support chat to solve your issue.
