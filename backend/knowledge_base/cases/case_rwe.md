- **Founded / Founders**: RWE was established on 25 April 1898 in Essen as *Rheinisch‑Westfälisches Elektrizitätswerk AG* by Hugo Stinnes and the city’s mayor Erich Zweigert (with Erl.‑Actien‑Gesellschaft vorm. W. Lahmeyer & Co involved initially). ([en.wikipedia.org])

- **Mission/Vision**: RWE positions itself as a "key driver of the energy transformation," aiming to deliver secure, reliable, and increasingly clean electricity amid growing digitalization and electrification demands. It invests heavily in renewables and storage to underpin energy security. ([rwe.com])

 - **Employees**: Approximately 20,000 worldwide as of recent figures. ([rwe.com])
 - **Revenue / Net Income**: For 2024, revenue was €29.8 billion and net income €5.14 billion. ([en.wikipedia.org])

- **Funding History**: As a public company, RWE has no external venture rounds. Funding comes from operations and capital markets. [Not found in public sources]

 - CEO: Markus Krebber (in role since May 2021; joined RWE in 2012, previously CFO) ([ru.wikipedia.org])
 - Chairman of Supervisory Board: Dr. Werner Brandt (since 2016, former CFO of SAP) ([ru.wikipedia.org])
