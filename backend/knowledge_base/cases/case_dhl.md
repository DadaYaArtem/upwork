## О компании клиента (web research)
DHL was founded on September 25, 1969 in San Francisco by Adrian Dalsey, Larry Hillblom, and Robert Lynn—the company's name is derived from the initials of their surnames ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 DHL’s broader Group-level mission includes driving towards net‑zero logistics emissions by 2050 and focusing on smart growth, emission‑reduced logistics, and digital transformation as part of its Strategy 2030 ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 As of Q1 2025, DHL employed approximately **580,580** people ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 In 2024, the Express division’s 12-month revenue ending September 30 was about **$92.79 billion**, with the quarter ending September 30 alone at **$23.45 billion**; annual revenue for 2023 was **$91.50 billion** ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 DHL is part of Deutsche Post AG (DHL Group) and is publicly traded; it does not have venture‑style funding rounds ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 - Tobias Meyer serves as CEO of DHL Group ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - On the supervisory board, Dr. Katrin Suder is Chair and Melanie Kreis is CFO ([en.wikipedia.org]).
