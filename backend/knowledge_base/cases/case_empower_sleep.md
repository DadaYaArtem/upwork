## О компании клиента (web research)
- Founded: Public sources indicate Empower Sleep was founded in **2021**, with some mention of **2022** in earlier profiles. LinkedIn lists **2021** as the founding year ([linkedin.com]). 
- Founders / Leadership: Co-founded by **Sahil Chopra** (CEO, “Chief Sleep Giver”) and **Sagar Chopra** ([crunchbase.com]). 
- Mission/Vision: Their mission is to “enrich lives through the power of better sleep” ([ashrust.medium.com]). They aim to fix a “complex, expensive, and broken” sleep care system by providing data-driven, accessible, personalized virtual sleep care ([empowersleep.com]). 
- Company Size & Location: Approximately **13 employees**, per BounceWatch, and LinkedIn lists size as **2–10 employees**, with headquarters in **Los Angeles, California** (also noted as Irvine on Crunchbase but LA more consistent) ([bouncewatch.com]). 
- Funding History: Completed **one Pre-Seed round** on **January 31, 2023**, with investors including **Balaji Srinivasan**, **Ash Rust**, **Dorm Room Fund**, **Rough Draft Ventures**, **The MBA Fund**, **House Capital**, **Andrew Macdonald**, **Ankit Jain**, among others (9 investors) ([bouncewatch.com]).

- **Main Services**: Beyond telemedicine and referral portal:
 - Offers at-home **FDA-cleared SleepImage ring** tests with six-channel data (plethysmogram, heart rate/variability, respiration, SpO₂, actigraphy); over **90.5% accuracy** vs. lab tests; severe sleep apnea detection accuracy **98.8%**, mild **96.3%** ([empowersleep.com]).
 - Program tiers:
 - **Sleep Foundation**: 1-month package for ~$1,200 one-time ([empowersleep.com]).
 - **Optimization**: 4-month comprehensive program for ~$4,000 one-time ([empowersleep.com]).
 - **Longevity**: 12-month full-year program for ~$8,000 one-time ([empowersleep.com]).
 - Transparent pricing options for insurance and self-pay: initial visit $199, 1-month program $500, ongoing membership $500/month, follow-up $149 ([empowersleep.com]).
 - Integrates with consumer wearables like **Oura** and **Apple Watch**; encourages these but emphasizes FDA-cleared solutions ([empowersleep.com]).

- **Target customers**: Individuals seeking diagnosis/treatment of insomnia or sleep apnea from home, avoiding labs; also referring doctors and dentists using the provider portal ([empowersleep.com]).

 - Collaboration with **Vivos Therapeutics** (NASDAQ: VVOS) for OSA diagnosis support, leveraging SleepImage and VivoScore devices for telemedicine and dentist referrals (announced Dec 15, 2022) ([vivos.com]).

- **Tech stack**: Uses FDA-cleared **SleepImage** system (ring and SaMD software) for sleep data collection and analysis ([empowersleep.com]).
- **Engineering team size**: Not directly stated; inferred from company size (~13 employees) that engineering team is small — **[Not found]**.
- **Technical content**: Their website includes blog-style "Crash Course" articles such as on RLS, Dopaminergic medications, Sleep–Acne cycle; not strictly technical engineering content ([empowersleep.com]).
- **Provider portal**: Internal portal enabling provider login, ordering home sleep tests, consulting between MDs, RXs for CPAP or oral appliances, viewing patient records including SleepImage and CloudPAT data ([guide.empowersleep.com]).

- **Funding rounds**: No mention of additional funding after Pre-Seed Jan 31, 2023 ([bouncewatch.com]).
- **Partnerships**: Vivos collaboration announced Dec 2022 ([vivos.com]).
- **Product milestones**: Not publicly documented in 2025–2026; their website shows patient testimonials continuing into March 2026 ([empowersleep.com]).
- **Expansion**: Available in **45+ U.S. states** per website ([empowersleep.com]).
- **Hiring trends**: LinkedIn lists 23 employees total — one job postings summary available but no specific roles shown — **[Not found specifics]** ([linkedin.com]).
