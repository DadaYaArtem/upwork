# Restaurant Cashback

## Общая информация
- Клиент: Restaurant Cashback
- Локация: Dubai
- Отрасль: Food, Restaurants

## Проблема клиента
Cash back service for MiddleEst market

## Решение
The chain of restaurants requires an application for its Entourage member users. The idea behind this application is to attract new clients and retain them with the help of the loyalty program: The entourage visitor gets a cashback from his paid check. Entourage members should also be able to book a place and monitor the events in restaurants.

Задачи команды: React Native & PHP

## Ключевые фичи
- The application is not for all the customers. The access to the application will be granted by a manager only to those people who are bringing substantial income to the restaurants. Those people who will get the access to the application should be chosen by a manager by certain criteria.
