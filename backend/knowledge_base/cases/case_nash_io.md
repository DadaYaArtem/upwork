## О компании клиента (web research)
Nash (originally known as Neon Exchange or NEX) was founded in 2017 by five open-source blockchain developers spread across three continents ([partnerkin.com]).

 While a formal mission statement wasn’t located, the company's evolution reflects a vision to blend non-custodial crypto services with regulated financial infrastructure—delivering secure, compliant digital finance solutions in Europe and globally ([scamminder.com]).

 The startup has around **25+ team members** (as of 2022) and planned to grow "more than 2–3×" by year-end ([nash.ai]). No recent employee or revenue figures were publicly disclosed beyond that.

 - **Pre-seed (~Aug 2021)**: $125,000 led by Y Combinator ([news.crunchbase.com]) 
 - **Seed (~late 2021)**: $7.8 million led by Andreessen Horowitz (unannounced initially) ([news.crunchbase.com]) 
 - **Series A (Aug 26, 2022)**: $20 million led by Andreessen Horowitz with additional investors (Y Combinator, Rackhouse VC, 640 Oxford Ventures) for a total of $27.8 million raised including seed ([linkedin.com]).

 Founders include Mahmoud Ghulman and Aziz Alghunaim, referenced in their Series A announcement ([nash.ai]). Roles such as CEO or CTO are not explicitly cited in public sources.

- **Nash Card**: A payment card introduced in early 2025, enabling users to spend crypto and round-up purchases to incrementally buy BTC ([nash.io]). 
 - **Nash Bridge**: A secure cross-chain bridge across major EVM networks ([nash.io]). 
 - **Unified EVM Wallet**, **Swap improvements**, **Gas Bundle**, and **Cross-chain utility** were also delivered ([nash.io]).

 - **Q1 2025**: Launch of Nash Card, “Pay with (w)BTC”, Round Up features, Nash Bridge, swap flows enhancements, Unified EVM Wallet, and Gas Bundle ([nash.io]). 
 - **Q2 2025**: Solana integration (deposits/sends, DeFi via WalletConnect), Google Pay support, and readiness of the referral program; new office in the Netherlands for expanding functions ([nash.io]). 
 - **Q4 2025**: Neo2→NEX migration for protocol update, full rebuild of ID verification system, Contacts/Chats feature development, and L1 wallet support across chains for payments & Round Ups ([nash.io]).
