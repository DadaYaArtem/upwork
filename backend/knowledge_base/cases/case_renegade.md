## Проблема клиента
HIPAA Compliance issue - the product is ready to be live but out customers found that they didn't make the product in the way that it suits all HIPAA requirements & Лекарства для профилактики и борьбы с ВИЧ + веб-приложение

## Решение
Medications for HIV prevention and control + Telemedicine web application.  The renegade.health At-Home PrEP Test Kit is ordered by a user's healthcare provider or through the website by an independent telemedicine provider. The kit and the telemedicine visit are 100% free of charge. After sending back the samples in the pre-paid FedEx package provided, test results will be ready within 3 days, and based on the test results, if qualified, the client's Community Benefit Organization Partners will work with a pharmacy in the user's state to send the user his PrEP prescription directly. The patient will have his own portal on the web app as well as the provider. So that both patient and provider can see the info in their portals which can be available by login/password.

Задачи команды: React, Koa, Node.js, Typescript, Postgres, Google Cloud Platform
