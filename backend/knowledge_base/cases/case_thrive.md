## О компании клиента (web research)
Thrive Protocol was founded in September 2021 by **Daniel Jacobs**. ([thriveprotocol.com])

 Thrive’s vision is to “maximize value creation for all people and communities around the world” by reimagining economic, social, and tech systems to unlock both economic and community value. ([medium.com])

 Thrive currently operates with “over three dozen developers, entrepreneurs, grants experts and specialists, and crypto degens and regens,” which suggests a headcount between **36–50+ people**. ([thriveprotocol.com])

 Thrive has completed **two funding rounds**, with the latest being a **Seed round on November 1, 2023**. ([linkedin.com]) 
 Public investors include notable crypto venture funds such as **HashKey, BitScale, Youbi Capital, Lightning Capital**, among others. ([thriveprotocol.com])

 The primary founder and likely key executive is **Daniel Jacobs**, who also authored the company’s founding vision. No updated details on titles or additional leadership were publicly available. ([medium.com])

---

Thrive Protocol provides a platform for **grant and bounty funding** to crypto communities, offering **automated and milestone-based capital allocation**, **validation of contributions**, and access to a **talent network of builders and creators**. ([thriveprotocol.com])

 - **ApeCoin**: In 2023, with only 3% of treasury allocations, Thrive funded **5.3× more grants** than the ApeCoin DAO. In 2024, ApeCoin increased allocations to Thrive by **20×**. ([thriveprotocol.com]) 
 - **Arbitrum**: With 2% of treasury allocated in H2 2023, Thrive funded **2.1× more grants** than Arbitrum DAO, supporting programs like STIP, ARDC, ARPC, and Step, with **~99% of delegates voting to renew**. ([thriveprotocol.com]) 
 - **Optimism**: Thrive drove a **1,505% increase in contributing ambassadors** and exceeded research goals by **632%**. ([thriveprotocol.com])

 ([blockchainreporter.net])

 Thrive targets **crypto DAOs and ecosystem treasuries** seeking to fund and validate high-value contributions from builders and creators. ([thriveprotocol.com])

 In July 2025, Thrive joined the **Hedera Network** as a strategic partner to support ecosystem funding—providing grants in HBAR tokens and offering mentorship and technical assistance to developers building on Hedera in DeFi, RWA, and AI verticals. ([blockchainreporter.net])
