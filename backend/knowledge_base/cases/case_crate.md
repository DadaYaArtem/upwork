## О компании клиента (web research)
- **Founded:** The CrateDB project was initiated in April 2013; the company Crate.io was founded in June 2013 in Dornbirn, Austria by Christian Lutz, Bernd Dorn, and Jodok Batlogg ([en.wikipedia.org]).
- **Mission/Vision:** According to a June 2025 “View From the Top” by CEO Lars Färnström, CrateDB aims to empower organizations with a real-time Unified Data Layer for analytics, search, and AI—streamlining workflows by consolidating disparate systems ([dbta.com]).
- **Company Size:** In 2019, Crate.io had around 26 employees in Austria, with planned hiring for 2020 ([vol.at]). In 2017, they had approximately ~25 people global total ([guug.de]).
- **Leadership:** In September 2020, founding CTO Bernd Dorn returned to his CTO role, joining alongside Uwe Weimer as Head of Sales EMEA ([cratedb.com]). As of 2025, Lars Färnström serves as CEO ([de.wikipedia.org]).

 - April 2014: €900k seed ([employbl.com]).
 - Oct 2015: €2.6M seed ([employbl.com]).
 - Jan 2017: €2.5M seed ([finsmes.com]).
 - June 2018: $11M Series A ([cratedb.com]).
 - Sep 2020: Another Series A of $14M (noted on LinkedIn) ([linkedin.com]).
 - Total raised approx. $31M by June 2021 ([cratedb.com]).

- **CrateDB 6.0** released on October 2, 2025. Key enhancements include migration to Lucene 10.2, optimized SELECT COUNT and join performance, improved nested-array schema support, stronger PostgreSQL compatibility, and advanced monitoring tools ([cratedb.com]). 
 - Additional stable releases: 6.0.5 (Dec 11, 2025), 6.1.x and 6.2.0–6.2.2 between Jan–Mar 2026 ([releasealert.dev]). 
 - CrateDB 5.10. series had multiple releases: 5.10.9 (Jun 16), 5.10.16 (Dec 10, 2025) ([releasealert.dev]).
- **Target Customers / Use Cases:** Positioned strongly in IIoT, manufacturing, real-time machine data (Industrial). Trusted by global manufacturers like ALPLA and ABB ([plattformindustrie40.at]).
- **Integrations/Partnerships:** Partnerships include AWS, Azure, IBM cloud; systems integrators like Accenture, Cap Gemini; ISVs/OEMs like Nokia, McAfee; integrations with Kafka, Flink, Docker, Kubernetes, Power BI, StreamSets, Pentaho, ZoomData, Telegraf; Microsoft co-sell agreement ([bloorresearch.com]).

- **Pricing Model:** Not explicitly publicized beyond offering Enterprise license and managed DBaaS on AWS/Azure ([bloorresearch.com]).

- **Tech Stack:** CrateDB is written in Java, uses Lucene under the hood, shared-nothing architecture; integrates components from Trino, Lucene, Elasticsearch, Netty ([en.wikipedia.org]).
- **Engineering Team Size:** Approx. ~25 globally as of 2017 ([guug.de]); Austria-only around 26 in 2019 ([vol.at]).
- **Open Source Contributions:** Migration of Enterprise features into open-source from v4.5 (March 2021) ([cratedb.com]).
 - Hosted webinars/events in EU: City tours in Vienna (Feb 2026), Amsterdam (Dec 2025), Munich (Nov 2025) with AWS ([cratedb.com]). 
 - A “From Sensors to Dashboards” webinar in Jan 2026 ([cratedb.com]).
 - Featured on DBTA (“View From the Top”, Jun 2025) ([dbta.com]).

- **Product Milestones:** Release of CrateDB 6.0 in October 2025; further minor/patch versions up to 6.2.2 by March 2026 ([cratedb.com]).
- **Events & Community Engagement:** EU City tour events early 2026, plus webinars in late 2025–early 2026 ([cratedb.com]).
- **Leadership:** CEO is Lars Färnström (since at least 2025) ([dbta.com]).
- **Hiring Trends:** Public job posting for Technical Product Marketing Manager - Database Solutions in Europe (September 2024) ([reddit.com]).
- **Support Info:** CrateDB support drop-off: version 5.7.0 EOL Apr 5, 2025; typical support duration is one year post-release ([cratedb.com]).
