## Ключевые фичи
- It`s a new business idea as such
- not widely presented on the market. The main essence of the project is the use of artificial intelligence to generate custom jewelry designs (earrings
- rings
- chains
- pendants
- charms
- etc.) on a website. Users can later customize these designs by choosing materials (gold
- silver
- stones
- etc.)
- size
- length
- etc.
- and then purchase them.
- Additionally
- people can create their collections of generated jewelry
- which other users can subscribe to and buy
- with the option to slightly modify the size
- material
- etc. Users can also become sellers on the platform. Manufacturer pages within the platform offer a range of products for purchase. It's also possible to generate new designs based on others' designs
- similar to a collaboration.
