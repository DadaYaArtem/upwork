## Решение
Women-led digital platform providing secure, user-friendly access to expert therapists and mental health support for teens and adults.

Задачи команды: UI/UX improvements

## Ключевые фичи
- Mintea is a platform for teens & adult users to identify gaps in their well-being and access quality resources to enable effective positive changes.
- Mintea provides a continuum of education and support from maintaining well-being to all stages of clinical care
- including prevention
- diagnosis
- primary treatment
- supplement to in-person therapy
- and post-treatment condition management.


## О компании клиента (web research)
- **Founded**: Mintea was founded on **September 21, 2022** in Dubai, UAE ([crunchbase.com]). 
- **Founders/Key Leadership**: The founding team includes **Alina Vasilache**, **Emine Yilmaz Butun**, **Michele Haus Karahan**, **Nail Murat Yalcin**, and **Sarper Tanli** ([crunchbase.com]).
 - **Mission**: “To make mental health understanding a natural, judgment‑free part of life — everywhere.” 
 - **Vision**: “A world where mental health knowledge is as common as physical health advice — no stigma, no shame, no borders.” ([mymintea.com]).

- Mintea’s platform offers four integrated pillars: mental health education & awareness, app-assisted self-therapy, direct access to licensed therapists (via text/video consultations), and an e-shop offering nootropics & supplements ([crunchbase.com]).
