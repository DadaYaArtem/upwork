## Проблема клиента
AcneAway is a New York-based startup that has been founded by two men who strive to bring the difference into the skincare space. When David, a businessman with a lot of ideas and dedication, partnered with a Joel – dermatologist that specializes in treating acne, they came to us with a bright and promising idea and asked the Interexy team to create a telemedicine solution for their business.

## Решение
The services for online communication and diagnosis patients and doctor in the sphere of acne care.

Задачи команды: We've developed a web version (MVP) - web development from scratch

- Achecare sphere. Connection to pharmacies. Compliances to all medical regulations.
