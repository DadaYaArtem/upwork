## О компании клиента (web research)
Public description indicates the platform supports assistance for individuals with developmental disabilities via integrated remote monitoring, smart notifications, alert services, IoT device integration, mood tracking, routine/task management, emergency support, caregiver communication, and use of Meraki sensors to detect environmental changes (e.g., doors, humidity/temperature, movement) ([nakforcreating.com]).

 The platform targets individuals with developmental disabilities and their caregivers/family members. It also addresses housing system gaps by integrating support into residential environments ([nakforcreating.com]).

 - Integration with **Meraki sensors** for environmental monitoring. 
 - Use of **Webex technology** for communication features ([nakforcreating.com]).
