## Проблема клиента
Need a user-friendly interface to create workouts of different levels of complexity, together with artificial intelligence

## Решение
AI for serious athletes looking to enhance their performance, emphasizing a strategic and data-driven mindset throughout the training process.

Задачи команды: In development - 2 UI/UX

## Ключевые фичи
- Advanced analytics on physical performance can and should be an indispensable tool in helping set goals
- track progress
- motivate athletes
- and ultimately identify what workout paradigms will truly maximize an individual’s physical preparedness for competition.
- Offers a truly customized program that provides athletes with concrete information on what their physical goals should be if they want to reach a given athletic objective. And intends to ensure that athletes reach those goals more efficiently than any other available options by using data from thousands of athletes and millions of workouts to statistically optimize their path.
