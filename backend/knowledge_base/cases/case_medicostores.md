From the official site:

 Medicostores offers a suite of solutions including: 
 - **medicostores.com** – a B2B pharma marketplace connecting stockists, retailers, and manufacturers. 
 - **Medico Local App** – for local pharmacy connections and door-to-door delivery. 
 - **Medico ERP** – for inventory and finance management for pharma retailers. 
 - **Medico Delivery App** – facilitating medicine order deliveries from stockists to retailers. 
 - **E-Invoicing and GST Integration** – streamlined compliance and billing. 
 - **UPI / Memo 2 Payments** – dynamic QR code–based cashless payment system. 
 - **Geolocation Search**, **Custom Promotions**, and **AI/ML‑powered inventory and predictive analytics** ([medicostores.com]).
