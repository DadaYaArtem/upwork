## Проблема клиента
15 Seconds of Fame (15SOF) is a simple way for fans to receive their video appearances from live events. The 15SOF app will deliver users any appearance from TV or the Jumbotron, where 15SOF has partnerships.  15SOF has partnerships with pro sports leagues and teams, colleges and universities, broadcast networks, and various rights holders to deliver fans personalized content.

## Решение
The solution that helps to share videos with the visitors of various games on the stadiums ( Football etc.)
