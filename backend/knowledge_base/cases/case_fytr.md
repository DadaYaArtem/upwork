## О компании клиента (web research)
FYTR was founded in **2022** and incorporated as FYTR Inc. in **Brooklyn, New York** ([crunchbase.com]). 
 Founders include **Mark Jones** and **Ian Felder** ([crunchbase.com]).

 FYTR positions itself as a **precision nutrition marketplace** targeting fitness enthusiasts, especially athletes, offering personalized restaurant meals using AI/ML. Its stated vision: **"to make eating healthy as convenient as ordering fast food"** ([gust.com]).

 - Employee count: approximately **2–10 employees** across North America and Asia as of October 2025 ([leadiq.com]).

 - Estimated revenue: between **$1M and $10M** ([leadiq.com]).
