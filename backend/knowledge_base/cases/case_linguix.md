## О компании клиента (web research)
Linguix (legal name: Textly, Inc.) was founded in **2018** in **Miami Beach, Florida**. Founders include **Alex Lashkov**, **Vitaly Kukharenko**, and **Eugene (Evgeny) Godov** (some sources also list **Alex Buchmann**) ([thecompanycheck.com]).

 **Alexander (Alex) Lashkov** served as CEO until **June 1, 2025**, when he stepped down. His co‑founder and CTO, **Vitaly Kukharenko**, assumed the CEO role as of that date ([milled.com]).

 Employee count remains small: estimates range from **2–10** to **11–50** employees ([app.dealroom.co]). 
 Revenue is modest: reported at **around $14K per month**, equating to approximately **$170K ARR** at time of acquisition ([indiehackers.com]).

 - **December 2019**: Raised **$350K** in an angel round from **Nitrx Capital LLC** ([linguix.com]). 
 - **February 2022**: Raised **$1M** in a seed / pre‑seed round from **Grishin Robotics**, **Antler**, and **Flyer One Ventures** ([thecompanycheck.com]). 
 - **Total funding** approximates **$1.35M** over two rounds ([thecompanycheck.com]).

---

In addition to the grammar‑correction SaaS (web editor, browser extension), Linguix offers a **Writing App**, **Web Editor**, **AI Writing Coach**, **SDK/API** for integrations, and **mobile apps** ([thecompanycheck.com]).

 - **Linguix Learning App** (launched July 2025): features include **Romanization** for non‑Latin scripts, **live text feedback**, **haptic feedback**, **smart search with AI‑generated lessons**, and adding **Irish Gaelic** in October 2025 ([apps.apple.com]). 
 - **AppSumo update (Feb 2025)**: introduced **Tone Improvement Tool**, **Text Humanizer**, **Inline Translations in 14 languages**, **advanced AI grammar checker**, **enhanced paraphrasing**, **over 30 languages support**, and a **fast, accurate API & SDK** ([appsumo.com]).

 Began as B2C/freemium, with shift to **SMB and developer facing** via grammar‑check SDK/API ([indiehackers.com]). Products like the AppSumo offer emphasize both individual professionals and **team/enterprise** usage via business plans and team management features ([appsumo.com]).

 - Lifetime offers (e.g., via AppSumo) and **exclusive lifetime PRO deals** were part of promotional outreach ([milled.com]). 
 - As of May 2025, **60% discount promos** were offered to **students and military personnel** ([reddit.com]).

---

Public mentions include use of AI models (OpenAI‑powered paraphraser), browser extension technology, SDK/API, and support for mobile apps (iOS/Android). Specific languages or frameworks beyond previously known Stack are not detailed in job postings or tech disclosures ([appsumo.com]).

 **Vitaly Kukharenko** remains CTO (until June 2025) and then CEO, with a background in building technical teams and past ventures like BrainApps.io and SiteMarket ([theorg.com]).
