## О компании клиента (web research)
Garantme was founded in **2017** by **Thomas Reynaud** and **Émile Karam**. One source specifies it was created in December 2017 by two HEC Paris graduates facing guarantor difficulties during their own housing search ([cnp.fr]).

 The platform positions itself as a SaaS solution for real estate professionals to simplify and secure rental processes—focusing on digitalizing deposit, unpaid rent guarantees, document authentication, and GDPR-compliant services ([cnp.fr]). 
 Its motto “un monde ou la location se vit avec sérénité” (a world where renting is lived with serenity) suggests a mission to make rental access more tranquil ([bouncewatch.com]).

 As of late 2024, Garantme had approximately **100 employees** ([cnp.fr]).

 Garantme completed three funding rounds:
 - **Seed round** (2018) — investors included Fonds Ambition Amorçage Angels (amount unspecified) ([bouncewatch.com]).
 - **Series A** — approximately **$4.7M** (USD) raised around **September 1, 2020** ([seedtable.com]).
 - **Series B** — approximately **€15M** (or about **$16.3M USD**) in **January–March 2023**. Investors: Bpifrance, Bonsai Partners, 115K, Axeleo, NewAlpha Asset Management ([cnp.fr]).

 - **Thomas Reynaud** — CEO and co‑founder ([cnp.fr]). 
 - **Émile Karam** — CPO and co‑founder ([craft.co]). 
 - **Mylène Romano** — VP of Sales & Associate ([craft.co]).

The platform includes digital tools such as rental file management and certification, issuance of insurance approvals, and document authentication in compliance with GDPR ([cnp.fr]).

 No publicly available information was found on new features, products, or launches during 2025–2026. 

 Primarily targets **real estate professionals**—agencies and landlords. Also assists **international students**; one offering facilitates rental deposits up to €36,000, processed within 24 hours, tailored for students lacking guarantors ([installeasy.eu]).

 For student rental guarantees: about **3.5% of annual rent**, paid upon lease signing. No mention of deductible or waiting period; coverage up to €36,000; processed within 24 hours ([installeasy.eu]).
