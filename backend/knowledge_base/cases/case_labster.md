## О компании клиента (web research)
- **Founded**: Labster was founded in **2011** in Copenhagen, Denmark, by **Michael Bodekaer Jensen** and **Mads Tvillinggaard Bonde** ([techcrunch.com]). Some profiles note 2012 as the founding year ([thebrandhopper.com]), but the most consistently referenced year is **2011**.

- **Mission / Vision**: Labster’s mission is to **make science accessible for every student through immersive experiences**. Their platform is **evidence-based**, designed by real scientists to enhance student outcomes through virtual labs that connect science to real-world scenarios ([linkedin.com]).

- **Company Size**: According to LinkedIn, the company employs between **51–200 people** ([linkedin.com]). Buzzfile estimates about **33 employees** at their Somerville, MA location, with approximate annual revenue of **$9.3 million** ([buzzfile.com]).

 - **Series A (~2017)**: $10 million led by Balderton Capital, with participation from Northzone and Unity founder David Helgason ([techcrunch.com]).
 - **Strategic Growth (~2020)**: $9 million from GGV Capital and earlier investors ([edsurge.com]).
 - **Series B (April 2019)**: $21 million led by Owl Ventures, with Balderton Capital, Northzone, and Swisscom Ventures also participating; total raised by then was $35 million ([techcrunch.com]).
 - **Series C (April 2022)**: Additional $47 million from Sofina Group, Pirate Impact, and repeat investors (Owl Ventures, Andreessen Horowitz, GGV Capital, etc.), bringing total funding to approximately **$147 million** ([labster.com]).

- **Key Leadership**: Co-founders **Michael Bodekaer Jensen** (former CEO, now board member focusing on AI/product) and **Mads Tvillinggaard Bonde** (Executive Chairman) ([medium.com]).

- Labster offers **300+ browser-based virtual lab simulations** across biology, biochemistry, genetics, biotechnology, chemistry, physics, anatomy, and physiology ([businesswire.com]).
 - Through acquisition of **UbiSim** (October 2021), Labster expanded into **nursing and clinical training simulations** ([crunchbase.com]).

 - In **fall 2023**, launched an **Administrator Dashboard** to give institutional administrators insights into usage stats (time spent, average scores, etc.) ([businesswire.com]).
 - Introduced a **Customized Quiz Editor** allowing instructors and administrators to tailor quizzes, add original questions, and ensure accessibility via voiceover ([businesswire.com]).
 - Enhanced user experience with features like a **Success Screen** after simulations and improvements to the voice of their virtual pedagogical agent "Dr. One" ([businesswire.com]).

 - Over **6 million students** in high schools and universities across **100+ countries** ([businesswire.com]).
 - Serves **3,000+ educational institutions** ([businesswire.com]).
 - Clients include **MIT, Imperial College London, ETH Zurich**, and institutions in high schools ([techcrunch.com]).

- **Pricing Model**: Semester-based licensing that varies by number of simulations, institution type, and user base. Prices range from **CHF 1 to CHF 100 per student per semester** ([maef.ch]).

 - Integrations with major **LMS platforms** like Canvas, Blackboard, and Moodle ([medium.com]).
 - Some STEM visualizations included in **Google’s AR/3D asset project** ([edsurge.com]).

- Simulations built using **Unity game engine** with complex 3D interactive environments and embedded quizzes ([medium.com]).
 - Likely includes web technologies for browser-based delivery, though specifics beyond Unity are not publicly detailed.

- **Engineering Team Size**: Not explicitly stated. However, companywide size (~51–200 employees) and hiring trends suggest a sizable engineering contingent, especially given recent growth ([linkedin.com]).

- **Technical Blog Posts or Talks**: No dedicated technical blog or recorded talks located. **[Not found in public sources]**.

- No **2025–2026 funding rounds** found—last major funding was April 2022 ([labster.com]).
- **Leadership change**: In January 2024, **Michael Bodekaer Jensen stepped down as CEO**, with **Shawn Boom**, an edtech veteran, assuming the CEO role; Michael remains on the board focusing on AI/product development ([medium.com]).
- **Product milestones**: Fall 2023 launches of Admin Dashboard and Customized Quiz Editor ([businesswire.com]).
- **Awards/Recognition**: Named one of the "25 most promising education tech startups of 2023" by Insider magazine and winner of the 2023 Tech Edvocate Awards for best VR tools ([businesswire.com]).
- **Expansion**: Intent to expand in Asia and Latin America through 2022, leveraging Series C funding ([labster.com]).
