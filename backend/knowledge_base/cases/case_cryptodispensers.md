## О компании клиента (web research)
Crypto Dispensers was established in **2017** by **Firas Isa**, who serves as Founder and CEO ([cryptodispensers.com]).

 The company’s mission centers on democratizing access to Bitcoin, especially for unbanked or cash-dependent users, by providing transparent, compliant, and accessible financial rails through physical and digital on-ramps ([cryptodispensers.com]).

 - **Employees**: At least three leaders are publicly listed: Sabreen Rihan (Chief Compliance Officer), Aya Marin‑del Rosario (Executive Assistant), Jessica Hiller (Social Media Manager), and others, suggesting a lean team ([cryptodispensers.website]). 
 - **Revenue / Financials** (2025 operating snapshot): 
 - Total cash deposit volume: ~$3.16 million 
 - Wire transfer volume: ~$7.16 million 
 - Fee revenue from those rails: approximately $1.06 million 
 - Estimated operating overhead: $567K–$579K 
 - Estimated operating income before reinvestment: ~$482K–$494K ([cryptodispensers.com]).

 - **Founder / CEO**: Firas Isa ([cryptodispensers.com]) 
 - Additional leadership from “About Us” page: Sabreen Rihan (Chief Compliance Officer), Aya Marin‑del Rosario (Executive Assistant), Jessica Hiller (Social Media Manager), Logan Price (Content Writer), Tunde Abiola (Website Designer), Peter Giammanco (Strategic Technology Advisor) ([cryptodispensers.website]).

- Evolution from physical Bitcoin ATMs to multi-channel on-ramps, including in-store cash deposits (CDReload at CVS, Rite Aid, etc.), debit/credit card, ACH, wire transfers, and ATM networks ([cryptodispensers.com]). 
 - Multi-rail orchestration platform with direct API integrations to banks, payment providers, and crypto liquidity sources ([cryptodispensers.com]).

 - Cash-dependent users, unbanked populations, and others seeking easy access to Bitcoin. Also serves institutional and high-net-worth (HNW) buyers via “White Glove” wire transfers ([cryptodispensers.com]).

 - Fee structure: blended fee rate approximately 8.67% for cash deposits; 11% for wire transfers ([cryptodispensers.com]). 
 - First-time incentive: deposit $100+ cash to receive $25 in Bitcoin bonus ([cryptodispensers.com]). 
 - Volume-based pricing: higher monthly deposit volumes yield better rates and milestone credits ([cryptodispensers.com]).

 - Partnerships with retail chains: CVS, Rite Aid, others via CDReload ([cryptodispensers.website]). 
 - API integrations with regulated banks, licensed payment providers, and fintech / exchange partners ([cryptodispensers.com]).
