- web-sockets
- payments (stripe)

- admin panel

- **Founded**: Link City launched operations in **September 2023** in Morelia, Mexico ([onde.app]).
- **Founders / Leadership**: Specific names of founders are not publicly disclosed; however, recruitment of local entrepreneurs and driver group leaders contributed to team creation ([onde.app]).
- **Mission / Vision**: Not explicitly stated publicly, but the strategy reflects a mission to rapidly scale urban ride-hailing in smaller cities by forging strong driver trust and favorable driver agreements ([onde.app]).

- **Main Products / Services**: Offers combined services of taxi rides and food delivery in one app—marketed in Mexico as “Link City: Rides & Food” ([apps.apple.com]).
 - **iOS app updates** include:
 - Notifications center improvements (delivered even when the app is not running).
 - Redesigned “Get in touch” screen.
 - Removal of Firebase Authentication for simplification ([apps.apple.com]).
 - Focused initially on **Morelia**, a mid-sized city (population 200k–1M) in Mexico, targeting both locals and tourists ([onde.app]).
 - Achieved a **7× increase in monthly ride orders** in less than a year since early 2024 ([onde.app]).
 - App consistently rated **4.9** on app stores; average ride frequency is about **6 rides per customer per month** ([onde.app]).
 - Leveraged **Onde platform** for App Store Optimization, helping significantly increase visibility relative to competitors like Uber and inDrive ([onde.app]).
