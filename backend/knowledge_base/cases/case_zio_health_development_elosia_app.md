## Проблема клиента
An app for analises and urine test in the comfort of your home. HOW IT WORKS: 1. Receive your home urine test strips through the post. 2. Pee on an Elosia strip, wait 60 seconds and scan it with the Elosia software App. 3. Get immediate results based on your ketone, hydration, nutrition, vitamin C and electrolyte levels. 4. Use the data to make healthy changes to your diet and lifestyle, with the help of our dedicated support team. 5. Test weekly to map your progress and stay in tune with your body's unique needs.

## Решение
Adding new features & inmroving existing application
