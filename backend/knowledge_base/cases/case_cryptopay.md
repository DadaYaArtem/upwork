## О компании клиента (web research)
CryptoPay (registered as CryptoPay Ltd) was incorporated on **14 October 2013** in London, UK ([open.endole.co.uk]). Founders are **Dmitry (Dmitrii) Gunyashov** and **George Basiladze** ([crunchbase.com]).

 According to Endole, CryptoPay Ltd is classified as a **micro** private limited company with **under 10 employees**, and a **turnover under £1M** ([open.endole.co.uk]). 
 However, their ICO whitepaper suggests a wider scale: **56 full-time employees**, **450,000+ registered users**, **91,000+ cards issued**, **profitable and cash flow positive since 2016** ([files.cryptopay.me]).

 Public sources don’t list explicit revenue figures. Accounts are unaudited and turnover is “under £1M” per Endole ([open.endole.co.uk]).

 Funding data remains undisclosed. They raised via an **ICO**, but specifics (dates, amounts, investors) are not publicly documented ([bitgetapp.com]).

 - **Dmitry (Dmitrii) Gunyashov** – Co‑Founder & CEO (also sole director/shareholder/Person with Significant Control) ([open.endole.co.uk]). 
 - **George Basiladze** – Co‑Founder. Current executive role not specified ([crunchbase.com]).
