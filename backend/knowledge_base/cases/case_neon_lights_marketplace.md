## О компании клиента (web research)
- **Founded / Origins**: Neon Marketplace (often stylized as Neon Marketplace) was founded in **2020** under the umbrella of the **Procaccianti Companies**, based in Cranston, Rhode Island ([compworth.com]). 
- **Founder / CEO**: The company’s CEO is **Adi (Aditya) Dhandhania**, appointed in **April 2023**. He was also an early investor in the brand ([cstoredive.com]).
- **Vision / Mission**: Their vision is to reimagine convenience stores as upscale, technology-driven **food destinations**—a convergence of quick-service food, convenience retail, fuel, and EV charging ([cstoredecisions.com]).
- **Headquarters**: Located at **1140 Reservoir Ave., Cranston, Rhode Island 02920, USA** ([cbinsights.com]).
 - Estimated **9 locations** across Rhode Island and Massachusetts ([cstoredecisions.com]).
 - Employee count estimated **between 11–50**, roughly “40+ employees” ([compworth.com]).
 - Revenue estimates vary: approximately **$5.7M** per one source ([compworth.com]); other estimates reach **$10M–$50M** ([leadiq.com]); another source cites about **$6M** ([business.openmart.com]).

- Upscale food options such as **brick‑oven pizza**, **gourmet coffee**, **fresh sandwiches**, **drive-through**, and **touchless car wash** services ([neonmkts.com]).
 - Fuel services include **unbranded fuel** (flexible supplier sourcing) and **EV charging stations** (Tesla and broader partners) ([cstoredecisions.com]).
 - In **2025**, **EG America (Cumberland Farms)** began leasing and rebranding nine Neon Marketplace locations, effectively dissolving the brand in those markets ([cbinsights.com]).
- **Target Customers**: Geared toward **on‑the‑go consumers** seeking more than typical convenience-store fare—particularly EV drivers, commuters needing premium food, and hospitality-focused patrons ([cstoredecisions.com]).
 - Fuel model leverages flexibility by being **unbranded**, allowing opportunistic pricing ([cstoredecisions.com]).
 - Design/build partnerships include **Cordtsen Design Architecture** for exteriors and **Dash Design** (NYC) for interiors ([cstoredecisions.com]).

- The company employs a **modern foodservice management tech stack** combined with a **cloud-based back-office system** for convenience store, fuel, and accounting integration ([cstoredecisions.com]).
 - Launched a **mobile learning management system** with training videos accessible via employees’ phones ([cstoredecisions.com]).
- **Engineering Team Size**: No public data on in-house engineering team; statements indicate they **do not have a dedicated development team**, relying instead on third-party systems ([cstoredecisions.com]).

- **May 2025**: EG America (Cumberland Farms) began leasing and rebranding **nine Neon Marketplace locations** in RI and MA ([cbinsights.com]).
 - **March 2024**: Introduced a modern back-office and training systems, including separate food and c-store stacks → integrated accounting ([cstoredecisions.com]).
 - CEO Adi Dhandhania emphasized the importance of digital experience and streamlining labor in industry panels (2024) and company events ([cstoredecisions.com]).
