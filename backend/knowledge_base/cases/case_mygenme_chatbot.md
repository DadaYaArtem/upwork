## О компании клиента (web research)
- **Legal Entity / Parent Company**: MyGenMe is a project of **MJOB s.r.l.**, located at Buenos Aires Square No. 5, Rome, Italy. This appears in the website’s footer. ([mygenme.com]) 
- **Mission / Vision**: The app's messaging emphasizes becoming “your genius self,” offering personalized virtual assistance across life, health, work, and learning by continuously learning from the user. Described as “a key to unlocking your potential like you never dared imagine!” ([mygenme.com])

- **Main Products/Services (beyond known chatbot)**: MyGenMe presents itself as a **fully integrated virtual assistant** across domains—life coaching, health, work productivity, learning, content generation, negotiation coaching, educational support, technical troubleshooting. It positions the bot as a “super brilliant Avatar of yourself.” ([mygenme.com]) 
- **Target Customers / User Base**: The messaging suggests broad consumer application—daily tasks, health, work, learning. References to “used by your favorite companies” hint at some business adoption, though details are vague/confusing (mention of Landkit seems out of context). ([mygenme.com]) 
- **Pricing Model**: The app touts “100% Free Download” for a limited time, “like a rechargeable battery – you only pay for what you consume.” ([mygenme.com]) 
- **Key Integrations / Partnerships**: Mentions of ML, cloud synchronization, API compatibility, and GPT‑4 from OpenAI integration. Specific third-party partnerships not detailed. ([mygenme.com])
