## Проблема клиента
After a bad development team, Natalie Blackburn came to Interexy, and she first wanted us to just review the app they crafted. However, we quickly realized that the app lacked functionality, and we decided to create a new one from scratch. The CookBook has to become her main income, so we started the development as soon as possible.
