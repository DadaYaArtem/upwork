## О компании клиента (web research)
Roll originated around **2019**, co-founded by **Bradley Miles (CEO)** and **Sid Kalla (CTO)** ([prnewswire.com]).

 Initially positioned as a “financial operating system for social tokens,” Roll aimed to democratize monetization for creators via branded tokens, enabling tokenization, trading, and community monetization independent of centralized platforms ([prnewswire.com]). 
 A 2023 media article indicates Roll’s ambition to redefine the paywall space by offering a brand‑friendly alternative to adult‑content paywall platforms ([yahoo.com]).

 According to their LinkedIn profile, Roll is privately held with a team of approximately **11–50 employees**, headquartered in **San Francisco, CA** ([linkedin.com]).

 - Raised **$10 million** in a **Series A** round in September 2021, led by **IOSG Ventures** with participation from **Animoca Brands**, **Alchemy**, **Huobi Ventures**, **Weekend Fund**, **Mischief Fund**, **Audacity**, among others. Total funding since 2019 reached **$12.7 million** ([prnewswire.com]).
 - Investors also include **Galaxy Interactive**, **Hustle Fund**, **Gary Vaynerchuk**, **Trevor McFederies**, and **Balaji Srinivasan** ([prnewswire.com]).

 - **Bradley Miles**, co‑founder and CEO ([prnewswire.com]). 
 - **Sid Kalla**, co‑founder and CTO ([prnewswire.com]). 
 - Later articles mention an **Erik Zamudio** as CEO, illustrating possible leadership transitions or evolving public spokesperson roles ([yahoo.com]).

- Roll began as infrastructure for **social tokens**—providing APIs and bonding‑curve functionality for creators, DAOs, and communities to mint and trade branded tokens ([prnewswire.com]). 
 - Offered custodial products including a web app and Discord bot that supported token management, staking, minting, and memberships ([blog.tryroll.com]).

 - In **March 2025**, Roll announced sunsetting of its **web app and Discord bot** products, with full shutdown scheduled for **April 15, 2025**. Core non‑custodial functionality (staking, memberships, minting) remains supported across chains ([blog.tryroll.com]).

 - Creators, NFT innovators (e.g., Whaleshark), and DAOs seeking to tokenize communities. Roll supported over **350 creators**, including big names such as **Terry Crews** ([prnewswire.com]). 
 - Media reports reference Gen‑Z creators such as **Sommer Ray**, **Tana Mongeau**, and others using Roll’s fan paywall model ([yahoo.com]).

 - Early integration with **Uniswap v1**, enabling creators to set token bonding curves and liquidity parameters directly ([blog.tryroll.com]).

- Roll’s infrastructure is based on **Ethereum smart contracts**, supporting interoperability with DeFi protocols and enabling token trading and staking ([prnewswire.com]).

 - Estimated within the broader company headcount (**11–50 employees**), but no separate engineering‑only headcount publicly disclosed ([linkedin.com]).

 - A **Substack post (March 21, 2025)** detailed the sunsetting of the web app/Discord bot and retrospective insights on Roll’s history ([blog.tryroll.com]). 
 - No additional technical blog posts or public talks located.
