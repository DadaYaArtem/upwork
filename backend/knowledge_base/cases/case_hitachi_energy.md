## О компании клиента (web research)
Hitachi Energy was formed through a joint venture in July 2020 when Hitachi Ltd acquired 80.1% of ABB’s Power Grids division, operating as “Hitachi ABB Power Grids” ([en.wikipedia.org]). The entity was officially incorporated on June 30, 2021, and rebranded as Hitachi Energy in October 2021 ([hitachienergy.com]). In December 2022, Hitachi completed acquisition of the remaining 19.9% from ABB, making Hitachi Energy a wholly-owned subsidiary ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 The heritage of Hitachi Energy extends back to ABB's predecessor companies— ASEA (founded 1883, Sweden) and Brown Boveri & Cie (BBC, founded 1891, Switzerland)—before becoming part of ABB ([hitachienergy.com]). This legacy enriched Hitachi Energy’s long-standing expertise in power systems and grid technologies.

 While an explicit modern mission statement wasn't directly located, the heritage emphasizes double-fold roots—ABB’s grid innovation and Hitachi’s digital technologies—to drive decarbonization and energy transition ([hitachienergy.com]). Additionally, Hitachi's founding philosophy—"contributing to society through the development of superior, original technology and products"—continues to underpin its energy business ([hitachi.com]).

 Hitachi Energy employs approximately 50,000 people globally ([en.wikipedia.org]). Parent Hitachi Ltd reported about 282,743 employees group-wide and ¥9.8 trillion in revenue in fiscal year 2024 ([hitachi.com]). Revenue breakdown for Hitachi Energy specifically was not publicly found.

 - Andreas Schierenbeck serves as CEO 
 - Ismo Haka is CFO 
 - Gerhard Salge is CTO ([en.wikipedia.org]).

Hitachi Energy offers high-voltage equipment, transformers, and services for renewables and grid systems ([en.wikipedia.org]). Recent key launches:
 - **EconiQ Series**: SF₆-free high-voltage products. Replacing SF₆ with alternative gases can cut ~20,000 tonnes CO₂ equivalent for 10 bays of 420 kV breakers. Over 2,000 EconiQ units deployed and 15+ km of SF₆-free gas-insulated transmission lines (GIL) installed across 30+ countries ([hitachienergy.com]).
 - **Transformers using natural ester oil**: Over 1,000 natural ester power transformers and 130,000 distribution transformers deployed globally. In 2025, launched the world’s first 765 kV natural ester oil transformer in China ([hitachienergy.com]).
 - **HMAX Energy**: Launched at CERAWeek 2026 (March 23), this AI-powered service suite safeguards critical energy infrastructure, enhances operational efficiency, extends asset life across utilities, renewables, and data centers ([hitachienergy.com]).

 Emphasis on utilities, renewable energy operators, grid operators, data centers, and industries facing high electrification demand ([hitachienergy.com]).
