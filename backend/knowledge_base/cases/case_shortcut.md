## О компании клиента (web research)
- Shortcut Software, Inc. (formerly Clubhouse) was founded in **2014** in **New York City**, USA ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - Founding and leadership: **Kurt Schrader** is the Co‑Founder & CEO; **Shannjit Singh** serves as VP Finance; **Jordan LaCount** is Head of Product ([shortcut.com]).

 - Shortcut’s mission is to “ensure software development teams can do their best work,” striving to create "the most intuitive, lightweight and enjoyable project management platform teams actually want to use" ([shortcut.com]).

 - As of the latest data, Shortcut Software employs around **80 people** ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - LinkedIn lists their size as **51–200 employees** ([linkedin.com]).

 - In **December 2017**, the company (then Clubhouse) raised a **Series A** round led by **Battery Ventures**, following earlier seed funding from Resolute Ventures, Lerer Hippeau Ventures, BoxGroup, RRE Ventures, and Brooklyn Bridge Ventures ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - In **January 2020**, a **Series B** round was led by **Greylock Partners**, with participation from Battery Ventures and Lerer Hippeau ([en.wikipedia.org]).

---

- The core product is **Shortcut**, a project management/issue tracking platform with features such as story and sprint planning, kanban boards, roadmaps, objectives, teams, backlogs, and custom fields ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - Docs is a built-in documentation feature introduced in **July 2022**, enabling collaboration on long-form content within the platform ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 - In **September 2025**, Shortcut launched **Korey**, an **AI-powered product development tool** integrated with Shortcut and GitHub Issues. It automates creation of user stories, specs, sub‑tasks, sprint recaps, and supports natural‑language queries and real‑time updates ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 - Over **50,000 active organizations** use Shortcut, with **21 million+ stories completed** ([shortcut.com]).

 - Shortcut is **free for up to 10 users**, with paid tiers implied for larger teams ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 - Shortcut integrates with tools like **Slack, GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket**, and offers a **REST API** for custom workflows ([en.wikipedia.org]).

---

- The platform is built with a backend in **Clojure** and a front-end using a **custom JavaScript framework layered on top of jQuery** ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - Offers a **REST API** to access and manipulate project data ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 - Employee count is around **80 total**, but specific engineering headcount not delineated in public sources ([en.wikipedia.org]).

---

- No additional rounds found beyond Series B in 2020.

 - **Korey AI tool launched September 2025** ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - Launch of **Docs feature in July 2022** highlighted as milestone before that period ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 - In **April 2022**, Shortcut received **Great Place to Work Certification**, with 97 % employees rating it great to work for, well above average ([en.wikipedia.org]).
