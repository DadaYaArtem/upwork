## О компании клиента (web research)
Honeydew (formerly AcneAway, Inc.) was incorporated on August 26, 2020, and began business operations on March 5, 2022 ([bbb.org]).

 The company was co‑founded by David Futoran, who currently serves as CEO ([honeydewcare.com]).

 Their mission is to make acne care more effective and accessible, enabling patients to avoid delays in care. They emphasize building real patient-provider relationships through online care, rather than transactional quizzes or product sales ([honeydewcare.com]).

 Estimated employee count is approximately 36 employees, per industry profiling as of April 2026 ([startup-seeker.com]).

 No public information found on revenue figures or funding rounds for Honeydew/AcneAway.

In addition to online communication and diagnosis:
 - Virtual consultations with licensed dermatologists, nurse practitioners, and physician assistants via video.
 - Personalized treatment plans including prescription topicals and oral medications (e.g., tretinoin, clindamycin, spironolactone, doxycycline, isotretinoin / Accutane).
 - Management of acne and other skin conditions like eczema, rosacea, and seborrheic dermatitis.
 - Progress tracking via photo uploads, prescription management with shipping, pharmacy coordination, and insurance processing.
 - Membership and product fees are eligible for HSA/FSA reimbursement ([startup-seeker.com]).
