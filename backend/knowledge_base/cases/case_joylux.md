## О компании клиента (web research)
- **Founded / Founder:** Joylux was founded in **2014** in **Seattle**, by **Colette Courtion** ([bizjournals.com]).
- **Mission / Vision:** The company’s mission is described as empowering women's intimate wellness "from motherhood to menopause," delivering "science‑backed solutions" that normalize the once‑taboo topic of intimate health ([joylux.com]).
- **Company Size:** As of April 2025, Joylux had about **15 employees** ([bizjournals.com]).
 - Raised **$5 million** in an early **Series A** in January 2018 from investors including Alliance of Angels, Houston Angel Network, Belle Capital, Golden Seeds, and Sofia Fund ([joylux.com]).
 - In 2019, further Series A financing brought total funding to around **$12 million**, backed by Alliance of Angels, Belle Capital, Portfolia, Sofia Fund, and Kimberly Clark; this included placement in Gwyneth Paltrow’s Goop store ([joylux.com]).
 - By November 2022, it raised more, closing an **oversubscribed Series A** at **$13 million**, including an additional $5.5M from Silicon Valley investors J‑Ventures, J‑Impact, and Dr. Kathy Fields. This capital fuels international expansion and new product acceleration ([joylux.com]).
 - **Colette Courtion** – Founder & CEO ([bizjournals.com]).
 - **Advisory Board** includes notable figures such as **Dr. Sarah de la Torre** (Ob‑Gyn), **Heather Dazel** (VP Marketing), and a range of medical and business experts such as **Dr. Michael Friedman** (former FDA commissioner and oncologist) ([joylux.com]).

---

- **vFit** and **vSculpt**: at‑home handheld devices using red‑light LED, heat, and sonic vibrations to treat Genitourinary Syndrome of Menopause (GSM), stress urinary incontinence, vaginal dryness, and sexual dysfunction ([en.everybodywiki.com]).
- **Target Customers / User Base:** Midlife women facing intimate health challenges—from postpartum to menopause stages. Devices are sold direct-to-consumer and through medical professionals; also retail via Saks.com, Goop, and Neiman Marcus in the U.S., Canada, and U.K. ([joylux.com]).
 - **Essity**: Co‑brand and private‑label international partnership for Latin America and Europe ([joylux.com]).
 - **Goop**: Distribution channel in fostered placement ([joylux.com]).
 - Member of the **Female Founder Collective** to align with female‑led business networks ([joylux.com]).
