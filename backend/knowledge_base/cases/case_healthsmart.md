# HealthSmart

## Общая информация
- Клиент: HealthSmart
- Локация: Alabama
- Отрасль: Healthcare
- Формат: Outstaff

## Стек
- ReactNative
- Php

## Проблема клиента
It is a charity application founded by Rama Raju – a doctor at the University of Alabama, USA.

## Решение
Crossplatform development (RN - frontend, php - backend), разработка за 3 месяца.

## Ключевые фичи
- The application had to motivate poor people to take action and get back to a healthy
- active lifestyle.
- For this purpose
- we had to connect the application with Apple Health Kit and Google Health
- which collect all information regarding each user’s daily activities and create a table to estimate the personal results. After all
- the app compares data and chooses winners who get a certificate for free healthy food.
