## О компании клиента (web research)
- XBTO originated in New York in 2015. The founding team includes Philippe Bekhazi (CEO), Walton Comer (CIO), and Richad Idris (CTO) ([xbto.com]). 
- The idea emerged in 2013, with the firm officially launching two years later ([xbto.com]).

- XBTO aspires to create "trusted cryptofinance" and describes itself as a “micro ecosystem” combining trading, asset management, venture capital, mining, consulting, and emerging projects ([garp.org]).
- Their vision also includes democratizing money through liquidity, capital markets support, and a regulated, institutional-grade infrastructure ([garp.org]).

- One source indicates around 64 employees, with a New York office at 3 Columbus Circle ([hedgelists.com]). 

- In March 2026, XBTO secured up to US$217 million in capital commitments. This round included ValueLabs as a strategic technology partner and minority shareholder ([xbto.com]).

- CEO & Co‑Founder: Philippe Bekhazi ([xbto.com]) 
- CIO & Co‑Founder: Walton Comer ([xbto.com]) 
- CTO & Co‑Founder: Richad Idris ([xbto.com]) 
- Other executive leadership includes: Mimo Ousseimi (COO), John Liatos (CFO), Karl Naim (Chief Commercial Officer & GM Middle East), Olivier Chau (Chief Product & Technology Officer), Nicolas Genechesi (CIO – Investment) ([xbto.com]).

Beyond digital asset services: 
- Market making (spot, derivatives, OTC) across venues ([xbto.com]). 
- Asset management via XBTO Capital and the XCOF fund ([helpcenter.xbto.com]). 
- Venture capital through XBTO Ventures and Humla Ventures (Fund II, targeting layers and rails) ([garp.org]). 
- Crypto mining (renewable energy facility in New York) ([xbto.com]). 
- Tokenization and regulated stablecoin platform (Stablehouse) offering custody, payments, exchange and yield (up to 12%) ([xbto.com]).
