## О компании клиента (web research)
- **Founded**: Kollektiv Sports ApS was founded in **2020** in **Aarhus, Denmark**, by Danish Olympic triathlete **Helle Powell‑Frederiksen** and her husband **Ben Powell‑Frederiksen** ([eu-startups.com]).

- **Mission / Vision**: The company’s mission is to build a **Web3 ecosystem** that enables physical activity creators to create a **decentralized and community-led future of sport and fitness**. They aim to democratize access to high-quality training, mentoring, and community, integrating elite coaches into everyday athletes’ journeys ([eu-startups.com]).

- **Company Size / Revenue**: Publicly available data indicates that by the time of the 2022 funding round, they had generated **more than €160,000 in revenue** from their beta product and had users in over 55 countries ([eu-startups.com]). Employee-headcount or exact company size wasn’t publicly disclosed.

 - Pre-seed round of **€1.5 million** raised in **September 2022**, led by **Ugly Duckling Ventures** and **VF Ventures**, with angel support from figures such as Kerstin Mogull (former MD of Tate Group/COO of BBC Future Media) ([eu-startups.com]).
 - In **early 2024**, Kollektiv and its assets were **acquired by Svexa**, a sports AI specialist, to integrate its AI capabilities into Kollektiv’s training offerings ([sportsbusinessjournal.com]).

 - **Helle Powell‑Frederiksen** – Olympic triathlete, co‑founder ([sportsbusinessjournal.com]).
 - **Ben Powell‑Frederiksen** – co‑founder, with background in coaching and engineering/product; involved in shaping the Web3 creator economy model ([eu-startups.com]).

- Offers a **subscription-based digital platform** for endurance athletes (triathlon, duathlon, swimming, cycling, running) ([app.dealroom.co]).
 - Enables **creators** (coaches, pros) to form and scale digital communities with no upfront cost; revenue is derived from user subscriptions to these communities ([app.dealroom.co]).
 - Uses an **adaptive training algorithm**: personalizes weekly plans based on goals, time availability, subjective input, and **wearable data**. Also provides knowledge video content and access to expert advice, fostering community-led engagement ([app.dealroom.co]).

 - Endurance athletes across levels (beginners to elite amateurs) globally—users spanning over 55 countries ([eu-startups.com]).
 - **Creators** (coaches, professional athletes) who wish to build and manage communities digitally and monetize via subscriptions ([app.dealroom.co]).

- **Pricing Model**: Creator-led communities operate on a **subscription basis** for users. The platform itself is accessible without upfront cost for creators ([app.dealroom.co]).

 - Post-acquisition integration into **Svexa’s AI** platform to enhance multi-sport, AI-driven training protocols and digitized coach programs like Seidur and Ellida ([sportsbusinessjournal.com]).

- **Funding Rounds**: No additional funding after the 2022 €1.5M round was found; the key event was the **acquisition by Svexa in early 2024** ([sportsbusinessjournal.com]).
