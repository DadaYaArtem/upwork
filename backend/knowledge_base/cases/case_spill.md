## О компании клиента (web research)
Spill was incorporated in January 2023 in Delaware and launched publicly in 2022, co-founded by Alphonzo “Phonz” Terrell (former Global Social Director at Twitter) and Devaris Brown ([wefunder.com]).

 Spill’s mission centers on creating a social platform built by and for culture-driving communities—especially Black, Brown, queer, and other historically marginalized groups—to prioritize safe, authentic, and rewarding interaction ([forbes.com]).

 Spill employs approximately 2–10 people, per their LinkedIn profile ([linkedin.com]).

 Early reports suggest Spill generated approximately $1 million in annualized revenue by 2025, largely via advertising ([axios.com]).

 - Pre-seed funding totaling about $5 million raised, including a $2M extension round led by Collide Capital in late 2023 ([techcrunch.com]). 
 - Early seed round raised approximately $2.75M from Kapor Capital, Mac Venture Capital, and others, also around late 2023 ([forbes.com]). 
 - By early 2025, total raised exceeded $6M from VCs including MaC VC, Kapor Capital, Collide Capital, Greylock, and Bessemer, along with angel investment from Kerry Washington ([wefunder.com]). 
 - In February 2025, Spill launched a community equity crowdfunding round (“Own The Conversation”) with minimum investments of $250, attracting their users (“Spillionaires”) ([forbes.com]).

 - **Alphonzo “Phonz” Terrell**, Co-founder & CEO. Previously at HBO, Showtime, and Global Social Head at Twitter ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - **Devaris Brown**, Co-founder. 
 - **Kenya Parham**, Chief Growth Officer (promoted in late 2024). She previously served as VP of Community and Partnerships, and is a cultural strategist with notable experience in entertainment and political consultancy ([forbes.com]).

---

Spill enables visual conversations combining images, GIFs, video, and text—styled as “Spills.” It also includes features like:
 - **Tea Parties**: live audio/video chat events for community engagement.
 - **Spades**: played inside the app with over half a million minutes of playtime by 2024 ([forbes.com]).
 - **Stacks**: story panels or comic-strip style narratives ([forbes.com]).
 - **“What’s The Word”**: a daily word game with nearly two years’ playtime since launching in early 2025 ([linkedin.com]).

 - Introduced daily game **"What’s The Word"** in 2025 ([linkedin.com]). 
 - Equity crowdfunding campaign (“Own The Conversation”) began in March 2025 ([spill.com]). 
 - Spill achieved its first cash-flow-positive quarter by late 2025 and solidified partnerships with Netflix, Amazon Prime, and Paramount+ ([forbes.com]).

 Focused on Black, Brown, queer, and culturally influential communities—serving as both creators and consumers of cultural content ([forbes.com]).

 Monetization is driven through advertising and consumer insights reports sold to major brands ([wefunder.com]).

 Brand advertisers include Paramount+, CBS, Showtime, Sony Pictures, Amazon Prime, Netflix, and others ([techcrunch.com]).
