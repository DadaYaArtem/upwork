## О компании клиента (web research)
Elosia (product of ZiO Health Ltd) was founded in **2015** in **London, United Kingdom**, by **Neel Patel**, **Rory Ryan (biomedical engineer)**, and **Dr. Shaolin Liang (biosensors PhD)** ([startup.network]).

 The company’s early mission: offer a **subscription-based, at-home urine testing solution** for personalized health and wellness advice, leveraging patented biosensor technology to analyze biomarkers across various fluids ([startup.network]).

 - ZiO Health participated in VC accelerators including **SOSV**, **HAX**, and **Johnson & Johnson Innovation** ([longevity.technology]). 
 - As of 2022, it was in the process of **raising US$3 million** aiming to complete trials in the U.S., with plans to expand research capacity and grow teams in **China** and the **UK** ([longevity.technology]).

 - **Neel Patel** (Co-founder) 
 - **Rory Ryan** (Co-founder, biomedical engineer) 
 - **Dr. Shaolin Liang** (Co-founder, biosensors PhD) ([longevity.technology]). 
 No information found on current CEO or further leadership structure.

---

- Beyond urine testing, the underlying **ZiO platform supports therapeutic drug monitoring** via aptamer-based electrochemical biosensors designed to test **blood samples** in the U.S. ([longevity.technology]).

 - Initially targeted **health-conscious individuals**, especially those active in fitness/wellness, with a go-to-market via **fitness centers** (B2B2C), with later expansion into **B2C and partnerships with insurance providers** ([startup.network]).

 - Subscription model priced at **£8 per month**, providing **four dry-chemistry test strips** monthly; weekly urine tests deliver immediate biomarker readings and personalized advice ([longevity.technology]).
