## О компании клиента (web research)
Metrikus began as *Asset Mapping*, launched in **2012** by CEO **Bill Clee**, often emerging from initial work on Olympic Park infrastructure ([app.dealroom.co]). The company officially rebranded to *Metrikus* in **2019**, and the technology had been evolving since 2012 ([metrikus.io]).

 Metrikus aims to bring next-generation technologies to transform building efficiency—making workplaces smarter, healthier, more sustainable, and operationally clear by consolidating fragmented data into actionable insights ([metrikus.io]).

 As of a recent Platform Specialist job post, Metrikus employs **over 30 employees** across the **UK, India, Spain, Poland, and the US** ([metrikus.io]). Another report cites a headcount of **39 employees** and operations in **Spain, UK, USA, UAE, Japan, and India** ([metrikus.io]).

 Metrikus has raised approximately **$6.68 million** in a **Series A** round, per CB Insights data ([cbinsights.com]). Additional legacy data shows grant funding: €130k seed (2013), grants (2016), and £2 million seed in 2017 under Asset Mapping ([app.dealroom.co]). Furthermore, in **November 2025**, **Valereum Plc** invested **£0.6 million** in Metrikus via a strategic tranche-based deal to explore tokenization of their IP and platform revenue streams ([ph.investing.com]).

 - **CEO**: **Gary Cottle**, appointed in **April 2019**, concurrently serves as CEO of Valereum. He brings two decades of real estate experience, previously served as EMEA Head at Nomura, overseeing a £120 billion portfolio ([crunchbase.com]).
 - **Founder**: **Bill Clee**, associated with the Asset Mapping era ([app.dealroom.co]).
 - **COO**: **Michael Grant**, who has commented on the platform tech stack and partnership with Microsoft ([metrikus.io]).
