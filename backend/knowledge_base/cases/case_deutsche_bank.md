## О компании клиента (web research)
Deutsche Bank was founded on March 10, 1870, in Berlin, by Ludwig Bamberger and Adelbert Delbrück, originally created to finance foreign trade and support German exporters ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 As part of its strategic outlook, Deutsche Bank envisions itself as the **“European champion in banking,”** targeting leadership across key business segments in Europe, high returns, and a strong global presence supported by AI and a resilient business model ([db.com]).

 - Employees (2024): approximately **89,753** ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - Revenue (2024): **€31.5 billion**; Operating income: **€6.70 billion**; Net income: **€4.48 billion**; Assets: **€1.391 trillion (Q3 2025)**; Equity: **€68.7 billion** ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 Being a public, long-established bank, Deutsche Bank has no typical VC-equity history or funding rounds. It raises capital via markets and shareholder frameworks; no public equity round data applies. 

 - **CEO**: Christian Sewing ([en.wikipedia.org]) 
 - **Supervisory Board Chair**: Alexander Wynaendts ([en.wikipedia.org])
