## О компании клиента (web research)
Bitclear was originally established in **2016** in **Malta**, under the name Bitclear Ltd. ([bitclear.com]). In **2019**, it relocated and restructured as **Bitclear AG** in **Ruggell, Liechtenstein** ([bitclear.com]).

 According to Liechtenstein’s commercial registry, Bitclear Aktiengesellschaft was officially registered on **December 4, 2019**. Its share capital is **EUR 200,000**, comprising 2,000 registered shares at EUR 100 each ([moneyhouse.ch]).

 The board and executive team include:
 - **Christoph Markus Hasler** – Member of the Board, Managing Director, authorized signatory. 
 - **Markus Manfred Hasler** – Member of the Board, authorized signatory. 
 - **Peter Bruno Maissen** – Executive Board member, authorized signatory. ([moneyhouse.ch]).

 Current estimates place its headcount at **1–10 employees** ([crunchbase.com]).

 Bitclear articulates a vision centered on advancing **mass adoption of cryptocurrencies**, delivering a **secure, fast, transparent** financial ecosystem by building **scalable decentralized blockchain solutions** and ensuring resilience in financial infrastructure ([bitclear.com]).

Bitclear provides two main products:
 - **Bitclear PayGate** – A cryptocurrency payment gateway for merchants. 
 - **Bitclear Exchange** – For handling fiat-crypto conversions and transaction flows ([bitclear.com]).

 Their **Payment Gateway API** uses HTTPS/JSON to enable merchants to accept crypto payments and receive fiat settlements ([api.bitclear.com]). 
 The **Payout** feature allows merchants to send crypto (e.g., BTC) to arbitrary addresses, offering dynamic fiat-to-crypto conversion, payout limits, and statuses (e.g., NEW, QUEUED, SENT, EXPIRED, FAILED). Merchants can enable automated approvals via `autoApprove` parameter ([api.bitclear.com]). 
 Payment processing states (NEW, UNDERPAID, UNCONFIRMED, CONFIRMED, EXPIRED, INVALID) are supported with detailed state transitions and error handling ([api.bitclear.com]).
