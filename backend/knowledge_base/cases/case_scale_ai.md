## О компании клиента (web research)
Scale AI was founded in 2016 by Alexandr Wang and Lucy Guo while they were still students (Wang was 19, a student at MIT) and participated in Y Combinator ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 Scale positions itself as providing “humanity-first” AI and essential infrastructure for frontier AI projects, serving hundreds of customers across industries, from finance to government ([congress.gov]).

 As of 2025, Scale AI employs approximately 1,200 people ([en.wikipedia.org]). In 2024, revenue reached about $870 million ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 Meta Platforms acquired a 49% non‑voting equity stake in June 2025 for about $14.3–14.8 billion, valuing the company around $14 billion ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 - **Founders**: Alexandr Wang and Lucy Guo (Guo departed in 2018) ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - **CEO**: In mid‑June 2025, Alexandr Wang left to join Meta; Jason Droege (former Chief Strategy Officer) was promoted to CEO ([en.wikipedia.org]).

Scale offers data annotation, RLHF services, LLM evaluation, enterprise AI platform services, and operates subsidiaries Remotasks (computer vision/autonomous vehicle annotation) and Outlier (LLM data labeling) ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 - **Scale Evaluation (April 2025)**: A platform to test LLMs against benchmarks, identifying weaknesses and training needs ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - **Scale Labs (March 2026)**: Expanded research division building on SEAL, focusing on testing reliability of advanced, agentic, and multimodal AI systems; frameworks SWE‑Atlas and Voice Showdown ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 Notable commercial customers include Google, Microsoft, Meta, General Motors, OpenAI, and Time. They also serve governments: the U.S. (Defense Department) and Qatar ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 Partnerships include: 
 - U.S. AI Safety Institute (2024) for model evaluation ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - Five-year Pentagon contract (Thunderforge) announced March 2025 ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - Qatar government partnership (February 2025) to enhance government services via AI analytics ([en.wikipedia.org]).

- Founders (particularly Wang) have participated in congressional testimony and public talks emphasizing national security and AI ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - Jason Droege blog reaffirming independence post‑Meta investment ([fortune.com]).

- **May 2024**: $1 billion Series F led by Accel, involving Amazon, Cisco, Intel, AMD, and others; valuation ~ $13.8 billion ([techcrunch.com]). 
 - **June 2025**: Meta invests $14.3–14.8 billion for a 49% stake ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - Total funding over $15.9 billion across eight rounds ([owler.com]).
