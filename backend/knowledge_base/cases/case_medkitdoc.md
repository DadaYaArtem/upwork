## Решение
Telemedicine project for our clients in Germany.  They’ve raised 2 million euros in funding to continue the work on the back office of their solution.   Service is a telemedicine product for professionals who are constantly on the move and for certain business units in order to receive medical assistance quickly online.

Задачи команды: Mobile development from scratch (ios version)

- Наличие MedKit (это набор специальных приспособлений для оценки состояние организма пользователя

- стетоскопами
- приспособлениями для измерения давления - они соединены с приложением через Bluetooth.


## О компании клиента (web research)
MedKitDoc (legal entity: BDS Digital Health Solutions GmbH) was founded in 2020 in Berlin, Germany ([hoganlovells.com]). 
 Founders include Dorian Koch and Benjamin Gutermann, along with Patrick Bieg and Nicolai Nieder ([startbase.com]).

 The company’s mission: “To make receiving medical care as easy and fast as ordering an Uber.” ([app.welcometothejungle.com]). 
 Their broader goal is to improve access to medical care for elderly and chronically ill patients by combining the MedKitDoc app with certified medical devices and specialists ([eu-startups.com]).

 Estimates place MedKitDoc at **21–100 employees** ([app.welcometothejungle.com]). 
 Crunchbase, however, indicates 11–50 employees ([crunchbase.com]).

 • December 2021: Raised approximately **€7 million** (~$8.2 million) in a seed round from Vorwerk Ventures, Acton Capital, Picus Capital, and FlixBus founders Daniel Krauss & Jochen Engert ([eu-startups.com]). 
 • Total funding to date: between **€9 million** (~$10.2 million) ([eu-startups.com]). 
 • Crunchbase reports a more recent seed round on **Sept 11, 2023**, but with funding details obfuscated; investors include Health Wildcatters among others ([crunchbase.com]).

 - **Dorian Koch** – CEO, background as Team Lead at McKinsey & Company and IT Project Manager at Volkswagen AG ([app.welcometothejungle.com]). 
 - **Benjamin Gutermann** – Chief Medical Officer, with experience as GP and emergency doctor ([app.welcometothejungle.com]).

Beyond the telemedicine app and MedKit already known, MedKitDoc offers a combination of: 
 - App with certified video-consultation and remote diagnostics via Bluetooth-connected medical devices (digital stethoscope, ECG, etc.) ([healthcapital.de]). 
 - Full-service telemedicine offering to care facilities (stationary and ambulatory), facilitating high-quality care, reduced hospitalizations, and cost savings ([medkitdoc.de]).

 Specific product launches in 2025–2026 were not found. However, MedKitDoc's integration into a **Selektivvertrag** (selective contract) with AOK Nordost and BARMER to act as management company and telemedicine platform provider, embedded with GoMedicus physicians, signals an expanded service model ([de.linkedin.com]).

 - **Care facilities**: nursing homes, ambulatory care services, particularly for elderly, chronically ill, or mobility-impaired patients ([mybestes.com]). 
 - Partnered with over **50 care facilities in Germany** by end of 2021 ([eu-startups.com]). 
 - A pilot project (AOK Nordost) conducted across **seven nursing homes and ten practices** in Brandenburg and Mecklenburg-Vorpommern from June 2022 to March 2023, handling **179 remote treatments** and reducing hospitalizations and costs ([mckinsey.de]).

 - **Device integration**: Bluetooth-enabled certified diagnostic devices for remote vitals monitoring ([healthcapital.de]). 
 - **Partnership with GoMedicus Group**: GoMedicus provides telehealth physicians under new selective contract models ([de.linkedin.com]). 
 - **Payer collaboration**: Contractual relationship with insurers AOK Nordost and BARMER enabling integration of telehealth into healthcare delivery ([de.linkedin.com]).

 Between **June 2022 and March 2023**, **179 remote treatments** were conducted, achieving significant reductions in hospitalizations and wait times in nursing homes under a pilot with AOK Nordost. This was documented in McKinsey’s E‑Health Monitor (Jan 2024) ([mckinsey.de]).

 As of 2025, MedKitDoc now acts as the **management company** under a **Selektivvertrag** with AOK Nordost and BARMER, expanding its role in organized care provision and access to GoMedicus physicians for nursing homes ([de.linkedin.com]).
