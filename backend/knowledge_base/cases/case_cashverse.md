## О компании клиента (web research)
Cashverse appears to have been launched in **October 2021**, with the initial token **ADACash**, followed by **SOLCash** and **FortuneCash** launched subsequently ([reddit.com]).

 I could not find publicly available information on the founding individuals or current leadership such as CEO or CTO. 

 While there's no formal mission statement, Reddit descriptions suggest Cashverse aims to deliver multiple streams of passive income to token holders via staking, reflections, lottery systems, and P2E games ([reddit.com]). 

 No data could be found on employee count or revenue figures. 

 There are no accessible records of funding rounds, investors, or amounts raised. 

---

- **ADACash**: Launched first, supports staking with reflections in Cardano ([reddit.com]). 
 - **SOLCash**: Reflection token rewarding in Solana (non-stakeable) ([reddit.com]). 
 - **FortuneCash**: Third token launched October 28, 2022; lottery-driven token, 8% buy/sell tax (6% funds ADACash/SOLCash; 2% to team) ([reddit.com]). 
 - **Crypt2Date**: In-house portfolio tracking and profit/loss tool across wallets; subscription-based service ([reddit.com]). 
 - **SignalBot**: Telegram bot delivering security-checked investment signals across multiple chains, adjustable for risk ([quadrigainitiative.com]).

 - A significant security exploit occurred in **February 2025**: a BNB-based sandwich attack via a function in ADAcash smart contract, causing approximately **$107.9K** in loss. The contract lacked proper access control and slippage protections ([quadrigainitiative.com]). 
 - Additionally, in **February 2025**, Gate Research reported Cashverse among BSC projects incurring cumulative losses exceeding **$330,000** due to security incidents ([gate.com]). 
 - Security alerts by **SlowMist** highlighted suspicious on-chain activity involving BNB and ADAcash transfers in **February 2025** ([chaincatcher.com]).

 The offering targets holders of passive-yield tokens and users interested in P2E gaming and automated crypto tools via Telegram. Community outreach and giveaways (e.g., $25K NFT “Cashemon Eggs” giveaway) suggest engagement with retail crypto users ([reddit.com]).

 - **FortuneCash**: implements an 8% tax on buy/sell transactions, partially funding ADACash/SOLCash growth and revenue ([reddit.com]). 
 - **Crypt2Date**: described as a subscription-based portfolio tool ([reddit.com]).
