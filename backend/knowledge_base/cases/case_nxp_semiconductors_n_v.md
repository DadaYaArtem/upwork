## О компании клиента (web research)
- Philips sold 80.1% of its Philips Semiconductors division to a consortium of private equity investors—KKR, Bain Capital, Silver Lake, Apax Partners, and AlpInvest—in September 2006. The company was officially named NXP and launched at IFA Berlin on August 31, 2006. ([en.wikipedia.org])

- Explicit mission/vision statements are not prominently published; however, sources describe NXP's strategic focus on sustainability, edge processing to cut energy consumption by up to 50%, and leadership in automotive semiconductors and secure edge processing. ([nxp.com])

- Employees: Approximately 32,169 in 2025 ([en.wikipedia.org]) 
- Revenue: $12.61 billion in 2024 ([en.wikipedia.org])

- No traditional venture funding after spin-off; IPO completed on August 6, 2010 ([en.wikipedia.org]) 
- In January 2025, secured a €1 billion loan from the European Investment Bank (EIB) at ~4.54% interest, six-year duration ([media.nxp.com])

- First CEO: Frans van Houten 
- CEO from May 2020 to April 2025: Kurt Sievers ([en.wikipedia.org]) 
- New CEO effective October 2025: Rafael Sotomayor ([en.wikipedia.org])

- MCX microcontroller families with analog integration, wireless protocols, NPUs, and Ethernet support—targeting industrial, smart appliances, robotics, and intelligent edge use cases ([nxp.com])

- **January 2025**: Launched MCX L-series (L14x, L25x) – ultra-low power, dual-core MCU for battery-driven sensors (industrial monitoring, building management, flow metering) ([media.nxp.com]) 
- **March 11, 2025**: Announced S32K5 automotive MCU family—industry’s first 16 nm FinFET MCU with embedded MRAM—for scalable software-defined vehicle architectures ([media.nxp.com]) 
- **CES 2026**: Unveiled S32N7 “super-integration” processor series for intelligent vehicle core—5 nm-based platform enabling AI-powered, centralized vehicle software/data lifecycle ([computerweekly.com])

- Developers in industrial automation, smart appliances, renewable energy, robotics, smart buildings (for MCX series) ([nxp.com])
