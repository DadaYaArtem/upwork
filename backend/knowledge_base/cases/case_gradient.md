## О компании клиента (web research)
Gradient (API platform for AI developers) was founded in 2023 by **Christopher Chang**, **Mark Huang**, and **Forrest Moret**, who previously worked on AI products at Netflix, Google, and Splunk ([businesswire.com]).

 The company is based in **Burlingame, California** and reportedly employs **nearly 20 people** as of late 2023 ([businesswire.com]).

 Gradient’s mission is to **democratize access to AI**, enabling enterprises to build custom models using their private data. They aim to power scalable AI deployment via their "AI Cloud" platform ([businesswire.com]).

 No public data found regarding revenue figures.

 - In **October 2023**, Gradient raised **$10 million in Seed funding**, led by Wing VC and joined by Mango Capital, Tokyo Black, The New Normal Fund, Secure Octane, Global Founders Capital, and angel investors ([businesswire.com]). 

 - **Christopher Chang** – Co‑founder & CEO ([businesswire.com]) 
 - **Mark Huang** – Co‑founder & presumably CTO or Chief Architect (specific title not in initial article) ([businesswire.com]) 
 - **Forrest Moret** – Co‑founder ([businesswire.com]) 
 - **Mark Kim‑Huang** (possibly same as Mark Huang) is referenced as Co‑Founder & Chief Architect in a January 2025 blog interview ([gradient.ai]).

Gradient offers an **API platform** that allows companies to leverage private data to build and manage large-scale custom LLMs. It supports domain-specific models (e.g., finance, healthcare, law) and integrates with tools like Llama2, Hugging Face, LangChain, LlamaIndex, and Pinecone ([businesswire.com]).

 Usage is **on-demand**, with clients paying only for tokens consumed. For enterprises needing more dedicated deployments, there is an optional dedicated deployment model ([businesswire.com]).

 - Product teams, developers, and ML engineers in industries such as **healthcare**, **financial services**, **marketing**, **media**, and **entertainment** have used Gradient to deploy generative AI applications ([businesswire.com]).

 The platform is compatible with open-source tools and frameworks: **Hugging Face**, **LangChain**, **LlamaIndex**, **Pinecone** ([businesswire.com]).

Leverages state-of-the-art open-source LLMs like **Llama2**. Integrations include **Hugging Face**, **LangChain**, **LlamaIndex**, **Pinecone** ([businesswire.com]).
