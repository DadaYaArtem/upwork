 - **Track construction progress** through descriptions, photos, videos
 - **Manage documents** centrally (contracts, specs, etc.)
 - **Monitor payments** (completed/upcoming) related to apartment purchase and renovation ([kass-group.com]).

 The platform is clearly tailored to **first-time home buyers purchasing from contractors/developers**. It also attracts contractors and developers interested in adopting digital management for their buyers ([il.linkedin.com]).
