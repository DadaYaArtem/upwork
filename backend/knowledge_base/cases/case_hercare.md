## Общая информация

- Локация: Sweden

## Проблема клиента
Many of us women are bothered by recurring problems such as low mood, irritability, night sweats, sore breasts or low mood - the app is solving those issues

## Решение
Log your symptoms, see patterns, gain insight and receive advice based on just your symptoms and your cycle, directly in our app!  Many of us women are bothered by recurring problems such as low mood, irritability, night sweats, sore breasts or low mood. Unfortunately, many people do not know that their symptoms may be caused by hormonal imbalance and that there is help available

Задачи команды: iOS, Backend & Android part

## О компании клиента (web research)
- **Founded**: HerCare was founded in **2017**, headquartered in **Stocksund / Stockholm**, Sweden ([thecompanycheck.com]). Another source references a launch date of **2018** in Danderyd, Sweden, with an employee count of **11–50** ([app.dealroom.co]). 
- **Founder & Leadership**: Founded by **Mia Lundin**, a long-time practitioner in hormonal imbalance treatments, especially PMS, menopause, and premenopausal issues. She previously founded The Center for Hormonal & Nutritional Balance Inc., located in Santa Barbara in 2005 ([publish.ne.cision.com]). Key investors and leadership include **Per Båtelson** (founder of Capio and Global Health Partner) and **Ahsan Amjad** (founder of ABC Labs, early investor in Kry) ([publish.ne.cision.com]).
- **Mission/Vision**: Focused on addressing stress-related female health issues (like hormonal imbalances) via digital offerings and physical clinic. Mia Lundin emphasizes bridging traditional and modern care to improve women's long-term well-being ([publish.ne.cision.com]).
 - Size: **11–50 employees** (Dealroom) ([app.dealroom.co]).
 - Revenue & Profit: In 2024, generated **SEK 12.5 million** in sales with an operating loss of roughly **SEK 5 million** ([mainsights.io]).
 - Raised **2.8 million SEK** in April 2024 (~USD ~270k), valued at **12.8 million SEK**, investors included Captea Markets, Cedarstorm, Anteris, Anna Dyhre, Lotta Losmee, Maria Rankka ([nordicangels.com]).
 - Raised **SEK 27.7 million** (~USD 2.66M) in March 2024; investors include Per Båtelson and Zeinab Daugaard ([nordic9.com]).
 - Investment by **Captea Markets AB** (December 2020) to support scaling of digital offerings ([publish.ne.cision.com]).
- **Acquisition**: Acquired by **CMedical**, a Norwegian healthcare provider, effective **April 23, 2025**, integrating HerCare into “HerCare by CMedical.” The combined entity approaches **SEK 200 million** in revenue across Sweden and Norway ([cmedical.no]).
