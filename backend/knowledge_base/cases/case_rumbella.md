 Rumbella was founded in 2024 and is based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates ([f6s.com]). 
 Founding team includes Chris Wilson, Max Soe, and Ezzat Eid, with respective backgrounds in digital entrepreneurship, product/UX, and social entrepreneurship ([f6s.com]).

 Rumbella positions itself as a managed service platform addressing food waste via circular economy principles—aiming to reduce waste, cut CO₂ emissions, and connect surplus food sources with donation or composting channels to promote sustainability and resource efficiency ([rumbella.com]).

 No public records of formal funding rounds or external investors beyond listing founders. Rumbella does not appear to have publicly disclosed funding amounts or investor names ([f6s.com]).

 - Ezzat Eid – Co-founder (Serial entrepreneur focused on environment and poverty alleviation) ([f6s.com])

Beyond food delivery, Rumbella offers:
 - **Surplus Food Marketplace**: connects businesses with surplus fresh food to consumers at discounted prices ([walturn.com]). 
 - **Managed Service Platform**: tools for municipalities, retailers, producers to manage surplus, report data-driven insights on waste reduction, connect to donation partners or composting, aligning with UN SDGs (SDG 2, 4, 8, 12, 13) ([rumbella.com]).

 - The iOS app “Rumbella” was first launched on July 3, 2024. Its latest update was released January 9, 2025 (version 3.0.1+103) ([appshunter.io]). 
 - The app was later removed from Apple App Store and is currently unavailable for download ([appshunter.io]).
