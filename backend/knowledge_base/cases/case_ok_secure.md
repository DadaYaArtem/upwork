## О компании клиента (web research)
OK.secure was launched by Fast Finance24 Holding AG (later Fast Finance Pay Corp., OTC: FFPP). The initial launch occurred in August 2020 under Fast Finance24 Holding AG via OK.de Services GmbH ([fastfinance24.com]). In Q4 2023, OK.de acquired exclusive rights to the OK.secure software’s source code from a Gibraltar corporation ([otcmarkets.com]). 
 OK.secure aims to combine secure communication with financial tools—positioning itself as a seamless, privacy-first platform for messaging, crypto payments, and digital banking ([ok-secure.com]). 
 Public sources don’t list exact figures for employees or revenue for OK.secure or its parent Fast Finance Pay Corp. However, Fast Finance24 Holding AG is publicly listed (ISIN: DE000A1PG508), which indicates access to annual reports—though detailed metrics specific to OK.secure weren’t found ([fastfinance24.com]). 
 No public information was found about specific funding rounds or investors beyond the corporate structure under Fast Finance24 Holding AG and its subsidiary Fast Finance Pay Corp. (FFPP). 
 - **Ole Jensen** – CEO of OK.secure brand / Fast Finance Pay Corp.; also Chairman of the Supervisory Board of Fast Finance24 Holding AG ([ok-secure.com]). 
 - **Sören Jensen** – Head of Sales, with a background in digital transformation and IT strategy across retail and online sectors ([ok-secure.com]). 
 - **Sayed Iqbal** – CFO, overseeing finance, budgeting, and IFRS/GAAP reporting since June 2020 ([ok-secure.com]).

- Combined offering as an all-in-one platform including messenger, crypto wallet, DeFi trading, cloud storage, video/audio calls ([oksecure.net]). 
 - OK.pay: offers seamless crypto trading, fiat conversion, debit cards, extensive payment methods ([ok-secure.com]). 
 - OK.merchants: enables B2B merchants to accept crypto and fiat via simple API, low fees, no chargebacks ([ok-secure.com]). 
 - **September 10, 2025**: Launch of **OK.ai**, an integrated LLM-powered AI assistant within OK.secure for messaging, planning, everyday help—claiming no data collection, free of charge ([ok-secure.com]). 
 - **November 13, 2025**: Agreement signed to integrate e‑banking and VISA debit cards (OK.pay) into the app, with rollout beginning December 2025 and full activation by Q1 2026. Offers IBAN accounts and cards in EUR and GBP, top-ups via crypto or SWIFT ([fastfinance24.com]). 
 Primarily privacy-conscious users seeking secure messaging combined with crypto payments. Also expanding to merchants (via OK.merchants) and general consumers of secure fintech messaging services ([ok-secure.com]). 
 OK.de, the free email platform, has around **2.3 million users**, potentially serving as a feeder base for OK.secure ([otcmarkets.com]). 
 The core offerings are free. OK.secure and OK.de services are free-to-use. Any premium tiers (e.g., more storage) were mentioned only in generic Terms of Use around OK.de, not specific to OK.secure—no paid subscription details discovered ([oksecure.net]). Revenue stems from advertising, commissions on payments, and fees ([ok-secure.com]). 
 - E‑banking and VISA debit card integration under OK.pay beginning December 2025 ([fastfinance24.com]). 
 - OK.de partnership and ownership under Fast Finance24 Holding AG. OK.secure acquired its source code exclusively in 2023 ([otcmarkets.com]).
