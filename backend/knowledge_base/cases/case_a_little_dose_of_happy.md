## Проблема клиента
Our research-backed happiness guides, thoughtful tips, and practical tools make it easy to incorporate more joy into your daily life.  Ready to become a happier, more optimistic you? Learn more about us and join our uplifting community today!

## Решение
The current product is a mobile application that helps people to increase their level of happiness by completing tasks and filling out journals.There’re 4 main components that will lead a user through kindness and happiness: tasks, journals, social community, and calendar. People will complete tasks and earn points to achieve happiness status levels or “trophies”. Achievement of the highest level will unlock special discounts.

Задачи команды: Crossplatform app, ReactNative, PHP
