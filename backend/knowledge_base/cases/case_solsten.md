## О компании клиента (web research)
Solsten was founded in 2018 in Berlin, Germany, by Joe Schaeppi (CEO) and Bastian Bergmann (Co‑Founder and COO), who met in high school and previously collaborated on a Harvard Business Review article. ([solsten.io])

 The company’s mission centers on putting humans first—leveraging psychometrics and AI to build healthier, more mindful experiences that enrich both users and businesses. ([solsten.io])

 – Employee count is estimated between 51–200, as indicated by LinkedIn. ([linkedin.com]) 
 – Craft.co lists total funding at approximately $22 million. ([craft.co]) 
 – Annual revenue is estimated between $1M–$5M annually. ([contactout.com])

 – In 2022, Dentsu Ventures (Dentsu Group’s corporate CVC) invested in Solsten, backing core products like Traits, Navigator, and Frequency. ([group.dentsu.com]) 
 – In September 2025, Solsten secured growth‑stage funding via a debt financing agreement with Decathlon Capital Partners to expand Elaris and Emotionally Intelligent AI agents. ([prnewswire.com])
