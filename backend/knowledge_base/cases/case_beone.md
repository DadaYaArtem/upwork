## О компании клиента (web research)
- BeOne—formerly known as BeiGene—was founded in **late 2010** by **John V. Oyler** (American entrepreneur, CEO & Chairman) and **Xiaodong Wang** (Chinese-American biochemist) ([en.wikipedia.org]). 

 - The company is headquartered across **Cambridge, Massachusetts (U.S.), Beijing (China), and Basel, Switzerland**, with global operations spanning **six continents** and offices in **over 45 countries** ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 - As of **2025**, BeOne employs approximately **11,000** people ([de.wikipedia.org]). 
 - Reported **annual revenue** for **2024** was approximately **USD 3.8 billion** ([de.wikipedia.org]).

 - **Merck & Co.** provided early backing with a **USD 20 million** investment in **2011** ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - IPO on **Nasdaq** occurred on **February 2, 2016**, raising around **USD 182 million** (6.6 million shares at USD 24) with Goldman Sachs and Morgan Stanley managing; supported by backers like Baker Brothers and Hillhouse BGN Holdings ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - A **follow-on public offering in March 2018** raised **USD 758 million**, and a **secondary listing in Hong Kong in August 2018** brought in an additional **USD 903 million** ([en.wikipedia.org]).

 - On **November 14, 2024**, the company announced intention to rebrand as **BeOne Medicines** ([en.wikipedia.org]). 
 - The Nasdaq ticker officially changed to **ONC** in **January 2025**, and the company redomiciled to **Basel, Switzerland** by **May 2025** ([en.wikipedia.org]).

---

- Major products include **Brukinsa** (zanubrutinib, a BTK inhibitor), **Tevimbra** (anti‑PD‑1 monoclonal antibody), **Pamiparib**, and others ([zawya.com]). 
 - The company’s pipeline includes **Sonrotoclax, Tarlatamab, Zanidatamab, Blinatumomab**, and many others (e.g., BGB‑26808, BGB‑R046, BG‑68501, BG‑C9074, BGB‑43395, Xaluritamig) ([zawya.com]).

 - In **June 2025**, the company presented **Phase 1a dose-escalation** results for **BG-C9074**, an ADC (antibody‑drug conjugate) targeting B7-H4 protein in solid tumors—particularly breast cancer. Among **56 efficacy-evaluable** patients, **confirmed ORR** was **16.1%** (9/56), **DCR** was **73.2%**, and **CBR** was **17.9%** ([arabtimesonline.com]).

 - Operating programs such as **“Talk About It: Cancer and Mental Health”**, aimed at integrating emotional wellness into cancer care, offering mental health resources for patients, families, and providers ([beonemedicines.ae]).

 - BeOne actively pursues partnerships with over **45 global collaborators**; offering services in clinical development, manufacturing, regulatory support, and global commercial expansion—particularly strong in access to Chinese markets and scale execution ([beigene.com]).
